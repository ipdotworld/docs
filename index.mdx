## What you will find here

**ip.world** is a **culture markets** launchpad: internet culture becomes **tokens** you can trade, with fees that reward creators, holders, communities, and the protocol—not just a naked chart.

If you are trying to **use the app**: start with the **User guide** tab. If you are trying to **integrate** (auth headers, routes): open **Developers**.
