## Public webhook surface

ip.world does not expose a public webhook surface for third-party integrators. The Next.js app at `ip.world` only serves inbound web requests; it does not emit public event webhooks.

If you need an integration, open a request in [Discord](https://discord.gg/ipworld) with:

- The event you want to subscribe to (deposits, trades, verifications, UGC payouts).
- Your expected payload schema.
- Your signing-secret rotation plan.

## Internal webhook consumers

The Go API in the `base` service consumes inbound webhooks from payment and social providers. Those endpoints run on private infrastructure and require operator credentials - they are not available to third-party integrators.
