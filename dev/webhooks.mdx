## Public developer webhooks

There is **no** stable, documented third-party webhook surface exposed from the `ip.world` Next.js app for general integrators as of this writing.

## Internal systems

Background workers in the **Go API** (`base`) may consume provider webhooks (payments, social providers, etc.). Those endpoints live on private infrastructure and require operator credentials.

## If you need an integration

Contact the ip.world team via [Discord](https://discord.gg/ipworld) with:

- the event you want (deposits, trades, verification),
- your signing secret rotation plan,
- expected payload schema.
