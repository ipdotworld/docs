## What UGC is **not**

- Not the same as **trend owner** fee share.
- Not the same as **holder rewards** windows.
- Not the same as **referral points**—separate pipelines.

## Concept

UGC rewards pay creators who post about tokens/trends on **X** and **TikTok** when their posts qualify and when fee pools allocate budget. Engagement and referral-driven volume both matter in scoring (see engineering simulators in `dashboard/docs/FEE_FORMULAS.md`).

## Indexing (high level)

Product docs describe hashtag / keyword rules such as TikTok posts tagging `#canonical_name` and X posts containing `$TICKER` or a bracketed keyword. **Indexing ≠ automatic payout**—moderation can remove posts or ban abusers.

## Payout cadence

Notion product drafts cite **weekly** distributions (example: Fridays **12:00 PM PDT**) after graduation milestones. **Confirm the schedule banner inside the app**—if UI disagrees with internal docs, trust **UI**.

## Eligibility checklist

- Linked **X** / verified **TikTok** on ip.world,
- Meet impression floors shown in live rules,
- Pass moderation,
- Token meets pool eligibility (e.g., post-graduation in product drafts).
