## What UGC is **not**

- Not the same as **trend owner** fee share.
- Not the same as **holder rewards** windows.
- Not the same as **referral points**—separate pipelines.

## Concept

**UGC (user-generated content)** rewards creators for posting about tokens. Creators earn from **trading fees** when their content drives attention and engagement—separate from leaderboard **points**.

## Connecting socials (requirement)

1. Open **profile / settings**.
2. Connect **TikTok** and/or **X** (one account per platform; you can disconnect and reconnect).
3. Linked socials are **required** to become eligible for UGC payouts.

See [Connect X](/user-guides/account/connecting-x) and [Connect TikTok](/user-guides/account/connecting-tiktok).

## Posting so posts count

- **X:** include **`$TICKER`** in the post.
- **TikTok:** include the **token hashtag** pattern the app documents (often tied to canonical naming).

Indexed posts can surface on the token’s **UGC / feed** surfaces.

## Feeds (per token)

Each token can show a dedicated content feed. Typical **filters** include creator type, platform, and time window. **Sort** modes often include **Featured** (default, driven by a **virality score** that favors traction with time decay), **Newest**, **My posts**, and **Most impressions**.

See [Social feeds](/user-guides/explore/social-feeds).

## Indexing vs payout

**Indexing ≠ payout.** Moderation can remove posts or ban abuse; pools must have budget.

## Eligibility (checklist)

- Connected **X** and/or verified **TikTok** on ip.world.
- Meet **weekly engagement thresholds** shown in live rules.
- At least one **active indexed** post in the scoring window.
- Content passes **moderation**.

Eligibility **does not** guarantee a payout.

## Payout cadence and mechanics

Internal product copy targets **weekly** distributions (example: **Friday, 12:00 PM PDT**). Typical gating (confirm in-app banners):

- Token is **bonded** (post–bonding-curve milestone),
- At least one **eligible creator**,
- UGC pool has **funds**.

Scoring highlights from Gitbook copy:

- **Top creators** (example cap: **top 100**) share the weekly allocation.
- Score weights **impressions** plus **referral-driven volume**.
- Fee allocation examples: **12.5%** of **`IP`** fees and **25%** of **token** fees routed to creator pools (see [Tokenomics and fees](/user-guides/rewards/tokenomics-and-fees)—engineering files may refine exact numerics).

Distribution is **automatic** in product messaging (no manual “claim” step where the app handles it end-to-end—still verify the live token page).
