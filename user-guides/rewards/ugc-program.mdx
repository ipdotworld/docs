UGC (user-generated content) rewards pay creators from a share of trading fees when their X or TikTok posts drive attention and volume to a token. UGC earnings are separate from points, referrals, holder rewards, and trend-owner earnings.

## Requirements

You need a linked X or TikTok account to earn UGC rewards:

1. Open **Settings** on your profile.
2. Connect **X** and verify **TikTok**. One account per platform.

## How to make a post count

For a post to be indexed and eligible for scoring, it needs the token reference visible in the post itself:

- **X**: include `$TICKER` in the post.
- **TikTok**: include the token hashtag shown on the token page.

ip.world indexes matching posts and shows them on the token's feed.

## Feeds per token

Every token page has a **Feed** tab. Filters include creator type, platform, and time window. Sort modes are **Featured** (default, ranks by virality with time decay), **Newest**, **My posts**, and **Most impressions**.

## Indexing is not payout

Getting indexed means your post shows up in the feed and enters the scoring pool. It does not guarantee a payout. To get paid you also need:

- A connected X or TikTok account.
- An active, indexed post inside the scoring window.
- Posts that pass moderation (no spam, no abuse, no plagiarism).
- The token is past its bonding-curve graduation.
- The UGC pool for that token has funds to distribute.

## Payouts

Payouts run **every Friday at 12:00 PM PDT**. Top creators for each token share that week's UGC pool. Scores blend impressions with referral-driven trading volume, so reach alone is not enough - posts that bring traders earn more.

Distribution is automatic. Once you qualify, the payout lands in your wallet; there is no separate claim button in the common flow.

The UGC pool for a token is funded from trading fees on that token.
