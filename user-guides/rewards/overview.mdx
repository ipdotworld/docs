## Quick map

The sidebar label **Referral & Points Program** maps here because **invite attribution** and **points / leaderboard** are easy to confuse with **on-chain fee share** and **UGC payouts**.

- **Referral link + `?ref=`** and **quest / season points** → stay on this page for the big picture, then [Points and leaderboard](/user-guides/rewards/points-and-leaderboard) for tiers (**Normie → Legend**) and volume brackets.
- **Percent of protocol fees on referred users’ trades** and **owner / IP referral cuts** → [Referral program](/user-guides/rewards/referrals) (fee-share story, not the points table).
- **Social creators paid from fee pools** → [UGC rewards](/user-guides/rewards/ugc-program).
- **Wallets holding the token** → [Holder rewards](/user-guides/rewards/holder-rewards).
- **Treasury and votes** → [Community Treasury](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals) and [Community governance](/user-guides/manage/community-governance).

## Read this once

ip.world runs several **parallel** incentive systems—this is how **culture markets** pay participants without collapsing everything into one “mystery reward”:

| Program | What it is | Where you see it |
|---------|------------|------------------|
| **Points** | On-platform achievements + trading season volume + quests | **Leaderboard**, profile |
| **Referrals** | Invite links that attribute signups / volume | **Profile**, search dialog |
| **UGC payouts** | Social creators earning from fee pools | Token analytics / UGC surfaces |
| **Holder rewards** | Longer-term holder windows from shared pools | Token UI / dashboards |
| **Owner / treasury fees** | Verified owner or community treasury routes | Owner dashboard, proposals |

![Earning systems diagram](/images/diagram-earning-flow.svg)

## Disambiguation (please read)

- **Trend owner economics** ≠ **UGC creator program**. Owners earn through verified fee routes; UGC creators earn by qualifying social posts that drive attention and volume.
- **Points** are not the same as **on-chain reward claims**—points feed leaderboards and future programs as announced in-product.
