Points are ip.world's off-chain season score. You earn them from onboarding quests, trading volume, referrals, and engagement. Your total drives your rank on the [Leaderboard](https://ip.world/leaderboard).

## Tiers

| Tier | Points |
|---|---|
| Normie | ≤ 500 |
| Casual | 501 – 2,000 |
| Degen | 2,001 – 6,000 |
| Trencher | 6,001 – 15,000 |
| Legend | 15,001 – 30,000 |
| GOAT | 30,001+ |

Your tier shows on your profile and on the leaderboard.

## Onboarding quests

Each onboarding quest grants **10 points**, once per account:

- Link X
- Link TikTok
- Set your username
- Make your first trade
- Create your first trend
- Launch your first token

The app blocks claiming the same quest twice.

## Trading volume

Trading points are computed on your **cumulative season volume in USD**, multiplied by the bracket your current season volume falls into. The bracket is a flat multiplier applied to all your season volume at once - not progressive marginal bands.

| Season volume (USD) | Multiplier (points per $1) |
|---|---|
| 0 – 1,000 | 0.05 |
| 1,001 – 5,000 | 0.10 |
| 5,001 – 10,000 | 0.15 |
| 10,001 – 25,000 | 0.20 |
| 25,001 – 100,000 | 0.30 |
| 100,001+ | 0.30 |

The active season is shown on the Leaderboard page.

## Referral bonus

Referrers earn points worth **10% of their referees' trading-volume points**. Signup bonuses and quest points are separate.

## Community moderation points

Users who contribute to moderation earn points:

- **Token rebind request**: 10 points
- **Comment, feed, or take moderation flag**: 5 points
- **Trend curation flag** (duplicate, incorrect owner, wrong trend type): 5 points

## Where to see your total

The leaderboard shows global rankings. Your profile shows your total, tier, and breakdown by source.
