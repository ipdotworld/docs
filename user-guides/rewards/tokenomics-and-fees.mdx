## Why this page exists

Traders care **why** price moves; builders care **where** fees route. This page stays qualitative; deep formulas live in engineering references.

## Fee distribution (product summary)

High-level **destination** split from internal Gitbook / product copy:

| Destination | Share |
| --- | ---: |
| Community / treasury | 45% |
| Protocol | 25% |
| Creators (UGC) | 15% |
| Liquidity | 12.5% |
| Referrals | 2.5% |

Plain language:

- **Community / treasury** — token-controlled funds governed by owners or holders.
- **Creators (UGC)** — weekly-style creator payouts from trading activity (see [UGC rewards](/user-guides/rewards/ugc-program)).
- **Liquidity** — routed to pools and market structure.
- **Protocol** — platform operations and sustainability.
- **Referrals** — growth incentives ([Referral program](/user-guides/rewards/referrals)).

<Tip>
  Exact routing can depend on **verification state** and **token type**—treat the table as the default mental model, not a per-tx debugger.
</Tip>

## Graduation

Tokens **graduate** from bonding curves into richer liquidity configurations; the **exact market cap target** is **config / UI specific**. Read the **Graduation** meter on the token page rather than third-party summaries.

## Bonding curve (trader view)

New tokens launch on a **bonding curve**: price moves with demand, and a **bonding progress** indicator shows how far the market is from being fully bonded. Once complete, the token is **fully bonded** in the sense shown in UI.

## Who gets paid (conceptual)

- **Traders** pay swap fees like any DEX market.
- **Pools** split portions toward **holders**, **UGC creators**, **treasury / community**, and **verified owners** depending on token type and verification state.
- **Points** are an **app-layer** ledger for quests and seasons—do not conflate them with on-chain fee splits.
