Every trade on ip.world pays a swap fee. The fee funds the five destinations below. This page explains the default split.

## Fee distribution

| Destination | Share |
| --- | ---: |
| Community treasury | 45% |
| Protocol | 25% |
| UGC creators | 15% |
| Liquidity | 12.5% |
| Referrals | 2.5% |

What each destination funds:

- **Community treasury** - token-controlled funds governed by the trend owner or, for community trends, by holders. See [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals).
- **Protocol** - platform operations, development, and infrastructure.
- **UGC creators** - the pool that pays the weekly UGC Friday distribution. See [UGC rewards](/user-guides/rewards/ugc-program).
- **Liquidity** - re-added to the pool to deepen the market.
- **Referrals** - pays signup referrers (5% of the protocol share) and trend-owner referrers (2.5% of the protocol share).

Holder rewards come out of the swap fee on a per-token basis: **30% of the token-side fee** and **50% of the IP-side fee** go to holders. See [Holder rewards](/user-guides/rewards/holder-rewards).

## Bonding curves

Every token launches on a **bonding curve**: price moves deterministically with demand, and early buyers pay less than later buyers. The token's bonding-curve progress is visible on the token page as a progress bar.

## Graduation

When the bonding curve fills, the token **graduates** from the curve into a deeper, Uniswap-style liquidity pool. After graduation, price is set by AMM trading, not by the curve.

Graduation is also the point at which most reward programs turn on - UGC payouts and holder rewards both require the token to be past graduation.

## Trend-owner allocation

Verified trend owners receive **3% of the supply** of every token under their trend, vested linearly over **3 months**. This allocation vests to the owner's wallet after verification completes.
