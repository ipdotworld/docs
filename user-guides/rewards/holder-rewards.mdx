## Mental model (Gitbook / product)

**Holding** tokens can generate **passive** rewards from trading fees: **no staking or lockups** in the product story—rewards **scale with holdings**, and **higher volume** on the market generally means **larger** holder-facing pools over time.

This is separate from **UGC** (creators) and **points** (leaderboard).

## What the app shows

Follow any **holder** panels, countdowns, windows, or claim CTAs rendered on the token page—those strings are closer to truth than long-form essays.

## Engineering detail (optional read)

Implementation uses **windows**, **snapshots**, and scoring rules documented in `base/docs/REWARD_SYSTEM_TECHNICAL.md` (for example **top-N** payout caps discussed around **50** wallets). Parameters can change—treat that file as **implementation reference**, not a promise of UI exposure.
