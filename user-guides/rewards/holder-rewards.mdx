## Mental model

**Holder rewards** split fee pools toward wallets that **hold** the token across scoring windows. This is separate from **UGC** (creators) and **points** (leaderboard).

## What the app shows

Follow any **holder** panels, countdowns, or claim CTAs rendered on the token page or dashboards—those strings are closer to truth than long-form essays.

## Engineering detail (optional read)

`base/docs/REWARD_SYSTEM_TECHNICAL.md` documents Blockscout/Storyscan holder snapshots, tracked hold time inside the system, and a configurable **top-N** payout cap (defaults discussed around **50** wallets in engineering notes). Parameters can change—treat this file as **implementation reference**, not a promise of UI exposure.
