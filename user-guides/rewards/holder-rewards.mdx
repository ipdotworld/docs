Hold a token → earn from every trade on that token. That's it.

**30% of meme-side trading fees** go to a holder pool for each token. The pool distributes to wallets that hold the token, weighted by balance. No staking, no locking, no wrapping — just hold it in the wallet you use with ip.world.

## How to qualify

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy the token">
    Trade on any token page. Your balance shows up immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay above the threshold">
    Each token has a minimum holding threshold for eligibility — check the **Holders** tab on the token page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the window to close">
    Rewards distribute at the end of each reward window. Balances are snapshot during the window, and eligible wallets receive an airdrop when it closes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where to track it

On any token page, look for the **Holder Rewards** widget — it shows your holdings, the payout timer, and rewards earned. Tap it to open the full modal with your projected share and claim history.

</Frame>

<Tip>
  Bigger bag = bigger share. More volume on the token = bigger pool. Both work in your favor.
</Tip>

## Holder rewards vs other programs

Holder rewards are **completely separate** from points, referrals, UGC, and owner/treasury payouts. You can earn from all of them simultaneously — they don't compete.

For the full picture, see [Rewards overview](/user-guides/rewards/overview).
