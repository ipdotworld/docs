Holding a token earns you a share of the trading fees generated by that token's market. You do not stake, lock, or wrap anything - holding the token in your connected wallet is enough.

Holder rewards are separate from UGC, referrals, and points.

## How it works

Every trade pays a fee. A portion of that fee goes into a **holder pool** for the token. At the end of each reward window, the pool distributes to the wallets that hold the token, weighted by balance.

- **Memecoin fees**: 30% of the token-side trading fees go to holders.
- **IP coin fees**: 50% of the IP-side trading fees go to holders.

Larger holdings earn a larger share of each window. Higher trading volume on the token grows the pool that gets split.

## Payout mechanics

Holder rewards distribute as airdrops, snapshot-based, on a per-token cadence. Balances are snapshot during the window; wallets above the eligibility threshold receive an airdrop at window close.

## What the token page shows

The **Holders** tab on each token page shows:

- The current holder pool size.
- The window timer and next distribution.
- Your eligible balance and projected share.
- Your claim history.
