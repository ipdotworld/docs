## What points are

`ip.world` tracks **points** for actions that add value on-platform (for example: onboarding tasks and referrals).

Points are visible in the app — you'll see them in:

- the **Leaderboard** page
- your **Profile** (including referral-related points)

## Onboarding quests

There are simple onboarding quests (e.g. linking socials, setting a username, first trade).

In the app, go to the **Leaderboard** and look for the Rewards/quests section.

## Referrals and points

Referrals can contribute to your points and show up as "referred users" with "points you earned".

See: [Referrals](/user-guides/growth/referrals)

## What we don't document here

We intentionally do **not** document internal point formulas, placeholder tokenomics, or anything that isn't directly shown in the app UI.
