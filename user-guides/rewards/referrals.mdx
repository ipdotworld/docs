ip.world has two referral programs that pay out on the same link: a **fee-share** program that pays a cut of on-chain trading fees, and a **points** program that rewards the leaderboard.

## Your link

```
https://ip.world?ref=YOURCODE
```

The `ref` parameter maps to your public username. Keep your username stable - changing it breaks attribution for everyone who signed up through the old link.

![Referral profile figure](/images/profile-referrals.svg)

## Trading referrals (fee share)

When a user signs up through your link and trades, you earn **5% of the protocol fee share** on their trades. This is real IP, paid out on-chain.

## Trend owner referrals

When a user signs up through your link and then verifies as a trend owner, you earn **2.5% of the protocol fee share** from the trends they verify.

## Points bonus

On top of the fee share:

- A successful signup through your link grants the referrer **100 points** once.
- Referrers earn points worth **10% of their referees' trading-volume points** on an ongoing basis.

<Tip>
  Fee-share referrals and points referrals run on different ledgers. The same signup feeds both.
</Tip>

## Tracking

Your profile shows your referral link, the number of users who signed up through it, their status, and your cumulative earnings from both ledgers.
