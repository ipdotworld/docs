## Your link

`https://ip.world?ref=YOURCODE`

The `ref` parameter maps to your public username / code—keep it stable so attribution does not break.

![Referral profile figure](/images/profile-referrals.svg)

## Trading referrals (fee share)

Gitbook / product copy: earn **5% of protocol fees** generated by **referred users’ trades**—a cash-flow style incentive tied to real trading activity.

## IP owner referrals

For **verified IP / owner** flows, product messaging cites **2.5% of fees** from verified tokens, with a dedicated call-to-action in **Profile** for owners who refer other IP onto the platform.

## Points and leaderboard (separate layer)

The app also runs **points** for quests and seasons:

- A successful referral that creates a new user can award **100 points** to the referrer (engineering default in `REWARDS_AND_REFERRAL_SYSTEM.md`).
- Referrers can earn **10%** of many downstream **point** events through the main reward service—see [Points and leaderboard](/user-guides/rewards/points-and-leaderboard).

<Tip>
  **Fee-share referrals** (percent of protocol fees) and **points referrals** are not the same ledger—read both sections above.
</Tip>

## Tracking

Profile surfaces referral links, copy buttons, and tables summarizing referred users and earnings where the UI enables it.
