Virality is ip.world's attention score. It blends social engagement, trading activity, and time decay into a single 0–100 number that ranks trends and tokens in Explore and powers the **Featured** sort in social feeds.

## How to read it

Virality is a **ranking signal**, not a fundamental metric. A high score means the trend or token is pulling attention right now - it does not predict price.

Scores decay over time, so a viral moment from last month no longer drags today's ranking up. Sustained attention requires sustained activity.

## Tiers

Virality ties into leaderboard rewards through four tiers:

| Tier | Score range |
|---|---|
| Tier 1 | 90–100 |
| Tier 2 | 75–89 |
| Tier 3 | 50–74 |
| Tier 4 | 25–49 |

Verified trends earn the full tier reward. Unverified trends earn a small fraction at tiers 1 and 2, and zero at tiers 3 and 4 - verification is what unlocks the full virality payout.

## Legacy note

Scheduled virality point awards from older releases are deprecated. Historical rows may still exist in the backend, but no new points flow through that path. Use the current Leaderboard categories instead.
