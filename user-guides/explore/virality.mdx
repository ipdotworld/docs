## What to trust

Virality displays are meant to summarize **attention** on a trend or token. The **exact score** may combine multiple backend inputs; unless a number is explicitly labeled as a formula in the UI, treat it as a **ranking hint**, not an oracle.

## Historical note for points hunters

Backend documentation notes that **scheduled virality point awards** are deprecated even though historical rows may still exist. Do not expect new virality **points** from that legacy path—focus on current **Leaderboard** categories instead.
