## What a trend page is for

A **trend** page answers: **what is this thing**, **who owns or governs it**, and **which tokens** exist under it.

## Key blocks

- **Identity** — name, art, description, category.
- **Verification / ownership** — creator vs community models show different cards; see [Creator vs community trends](/user-guides/verification/creator-vs-community-trends).
- **Token list** — pick a market to open its **token page**.
