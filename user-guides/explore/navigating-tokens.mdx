A token page is a single market under a trend. It is where you trade, read charts, inspect holders and transactions, follow social feeds, comment, and - for tokens with active governance - open treasury proposals.

## Header

- Token name, ticker, image, and description.
- Token socials: X, Instagram, TikTok, Telegram, and website.
- Verification badge, inherited from the trend.
- Endorsement pill, if the trend owner has endorsed this token.

## Chart and metrics

Market cap, liquidity, and bonding-curve progress toward graduation live above the fold.

## Trade

On desktop the Trade panel docks to the side; on mobile it opens as a bottom sheet. See [Trading overview](/user-guides/trading/overview).

## Anti-snipe errors

If the token launched with anti-snipe enabled, trades in the first 5 minutes that would push a wallet above 2% of supply fail with **Anti-snipe limit exceeded**.
