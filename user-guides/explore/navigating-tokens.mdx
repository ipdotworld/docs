## What a token page is

A **token** page is one market under a **trend**. It is where you **trade**, read **charts**, inspect **holders** and **transactions**, join **comments**, and—when enabled—open **Treasury** proposals.

## Header signals

- **Name, ticker, image**, and **description**.
- **Socials** attached to the token (including Telegram when provided at launch).
- **Endorsement** pill when applicable—[Endorsements](/user-guides/manage/endorsements).

## Metrics and chart

Market cap, liquidity, **graduation** progress, and chart modes are summarized in [Charts and metrics](/user-guides/tokens/charts-and-metrics).

## Trade entry

Use the primary **Trade** affordance (desktop panel or mobile bottom sheet). See [Trading overview](/user-guides/trading/overview).
