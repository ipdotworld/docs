Explore is the main discovery surface on ip.world. Three controls drive what you see:

- **Search** - text search across trend and token names, tickers, and aliases.
- **Category filters** - pick one of the eleven categories (Pet, Person, Trend, Game, Technology, Meme, Movement, Brand, Character, Art, Misc) to narrow the list.
- **Sort keys** - order by creation time, trading volume, market cap, virality, or bonding progress.

![Filters figure](/images/explore-filters.svg)

## Search shortcut

Press **⌘K** (macOS) or **Ctrl+K** (Windows/Linux) from anywhere to open search.
