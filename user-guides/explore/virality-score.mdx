## What it is

`ip.world` uses a **virality score** (0-100) to represent how much momentum an IP has right now.

The score reflects a mix of:

- social activity (X / TikTok)
- market activity (linked token performance)

## How to interpret it

Use this as a rough guide:

- **0-25**: low virality
- **25-50**: moderate virality
- **50-75**: above average virality
- **75-100**: high virality

<Note>
  The score is meant for discovery and context — it's not a promise of price performance.
</Note>
