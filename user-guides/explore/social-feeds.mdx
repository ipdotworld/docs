## What feeds show

On a **token** page, the **Feed** tab aggregates embedded posts (typically **X** and **TikTok**) relevant to that token and its trend—this is the same content family that powers **UGC rewards** when posts meet indexing rules.

## Filters

Desktop feeds commonly support combinations along:

- **Creator type** (who posted),
- **Platform** (**All**, **Twitter/X**, **TikTok**),
- **Time window** (rolling ranges).

Exact chips follow the live feed header.

## Sort modes

Product messaging calls out:

- **Featured** — default; prioritizes posts with strong **virality score** (traction with time decay),
- **Newest**,
- **My posts**,
- **Most impressions**.

Legacy builds may still show labels like **Oldest** or **Least Featured**—pick what your UI lists.
