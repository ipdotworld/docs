## What feeds show

On a **token** page, the **Feed** tab aggregates embedded posts (typically **X** and **TikTok**) relevant to that token and its trend.

## Filters

Desktop feeds commonly support:

- **All**
- **Twitter**
- **TikTok**

## Sort modes

Use the sort dropdown in-product (examples shipped include **Newest**, **Oldest**, **Most Featured**, **Least Featured**—verify labels in your build).

## Load more and refresh

Scroll to load additional pages when available; use **refresh** to pull the latest indexed state.
