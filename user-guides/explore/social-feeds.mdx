Every token page has a **Feed** tab that embeds X and TikTok posts about the token and its parent trend. The feed is the same content pipeline that powers UGC rewards - a post that is indexed into the feed is eligible for UGC scoring.

## Filters

Feeds support three filter axes:

- **Creator type** - filter by who posted (all, UGC creators, trend owner).
- **Platform** - all, X, or TikTok.
- **Time window** - rolling ranges (24h, 7d, 30d, all time).

## Sort modes

- **Featured** - default. Ranks by virality with time decay.
- **Newest** - most recent first.
- **My posts** - only your posts.
- **Most impressions** - by reach.
