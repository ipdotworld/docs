Every user-visible surface on ip.world has a **Report** action. Use it when content violates the platform rules, attribution is wrong, or something is spam.

## Where the Report action lives

- Token pages - menu on the token header.
- Trend pages - menu on the trend header.
- Social feed cards - overflow menu on each card.
- Comments and takes - menu next to each post.

## Report reasons

- **Inappropriate content** - NSFW, abuse, harassment.
- **Spam** - low-effort duplicate content or bot activity.
- **Duplicate trend** - the trend duplicates an existing one and should merge.
- **Wrong owner** - the owner link on the trend is incorrect.
- **Wrong trend** - the token is under the wrong trend.
- **Wrong type** - the trend is tagged as creator but should be community (or vice versa).

## Filing a duplicate-trend report

Pick **Duplicate trend** and search for the canonical trend the content should merge into. Double-check the ticker and the trend art before submitting - moderators use your pointer as the merge target.

## After you submit

Moderators triage reports from the moderator dashboard. You may not get a public response on routine reports. For urgent security issues (active scam, stolen account, impersonation of a verified owner), also ping moderators in [Discord](https://discord.gg/ipworld) with a link and timestamp.

Moderation reports earn **5 points** each for trend curation flags and comment/feed/take flags, and **10 points** for a successful token rebind request.
