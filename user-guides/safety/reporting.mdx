## Where **Report** appears

You will find **Report** entrypoints on token pages, trend pages (including feed cards), and individual comments.

## Common reasons

Options vary by surface but frequently include:

- **Inappropriate content**
- **Spam**
- **Duplicate trend** (umbrella should merge into another record)
- **Wrong owner** (creator attribution issues)
- **Wrong trend** (token linked to the wrong umbrella)
- **Wrong type** (creator vs community mismatch)

## Duplicate umbrellas

Choose **Duplicate trend** (or equivalent label) and search for the canonical umbrella the content should merge into—double-check ticker and art before submitting.

## After you submit

Moderators triage reports; you may not receive a public response. For urgent security issues, also ping moderators in [Discord](https://discord.gg/ipworld) with a link and timestamp.
