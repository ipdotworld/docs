## Moderated surfaces

Moderation covers user-visible content across:

- Trend creation (name, image, description, socials).
- Token creation (name, ticker, description, image, socials).
- Token comments.
- Takes.
- Social feed posts (X and TikTok cards).

Every surface has a **Report** hook so users can flag spam, impersonation, or NSFW material.

## Principles

- Keep the platform legible for traders: no duplicate trends, no fake owners.
- Protect individuals and brands from abusive derivatives when reported.
- Escalate edge cases through the moderator dashboard, not through public speculation.

## UGC enforcement

UGC payouts can be revoked for spam, plagiarism, or engagement manipulation. Confirmed violations can exclude a creator from future payout windows. See [UGC program](/user-guides/rewards/ugc-program).
