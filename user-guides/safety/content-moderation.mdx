## Surfaces

Moderation covers user-visible content across:

- **Trend creation**
- **Token creation**
- **Token comments**
- **Takes**
- **Feeds** (embedded X / TikTok cards)

Each surface has reporting hooks so users can flag spam, impersonation, or NSFW material.

## Principles

- Keep the platform legible for traders (no duplicate umbrellas, no fake owners).
- Protect brands and individuals from abusive derivatives when reported.
- Escalate edge cases through the moderator dashboard—not via public speculation.

## UGC-specific enforcement

UGC payouts can be revoked for spam or manipulation; bans can exclude future payout windows. See [UGC program](/user-guides/rewards/ugc-program).
