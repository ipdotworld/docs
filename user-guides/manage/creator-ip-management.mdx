## Who this is for

This section applies after you’ve successfully claimed a **creator IP**.

If you haven’t claimed yet, start here: [Claiming a creator IP](/user-guides/verification/claiming-a-creator-ip)

## Holdings & claims (owner dashboard)

As an IP owner, you’ll have access to management UI that includes holdings-style views for tokens under your IP.

Depending on the token and vesting/fees state, you may see:

- **Claimable** amounts
- already **claimed** amounts
- actions like **Claim**, **Sell**, **Transfer**, **Burn**

## Endorsements (signal to users)

Once verified, the IP owner can add endorsement signals to tokens under the IP:

- Official Token
- Liked By Owner
- Flagged by Owner

See: [Endorsements](/user-guides/manage/endorsements)
