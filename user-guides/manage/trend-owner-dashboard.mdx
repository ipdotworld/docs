The trend owner dashboard is available once you verify and claim a creator trend. If you have not claimed yet, start at Claiming a creator trend.

![Owner dashboard figure](/images/manage-owner.svg)

## Holdings and claims

The dashboard lists every token under your trend, with per-token:

- **Claimable balance** - the vested portion of your 3% allocation plus unclaimed fee share.
- **Claim history** - what you have already claimed.
- **Actions** - Claim, Sell, Transfer, Burn.

Claims are a **two-step flow**: first harvest the fees from the token contract into your claimable balance, then claim the balance to your wallet. The modal labels make the step you are on clear.

## Treasury

The dashboard pairs a Safe treasury with proposal-gated movements. Any spend larger than a trivial claim routes through a proposal so the owner action is auditable. Creators retain a cancel / veto rail on proposals submitted against their own trend.

See [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals).

## Endorsements

Endorse tokens under your trend to mark them as canonical.
