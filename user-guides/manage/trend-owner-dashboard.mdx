## Who this is for

You already **claimed** a **creator trend**. If not, start at [Claiming a creator trend](/user-guides/verification/claiming-a-creator-trend).

![Owner dashboard figure](/images/manage-owner.svg)

## Treasury and proposals

Owner dashboards pair a **Safe treasury** with **proposal-gated** movements. Heavyweight actions (claims, transfers, burns) should route through proposals instead of silent transfers.

Creators retain **cancel / veto** style safety rails on their own umbrellas—see in-app governance copy.

## Holdings and claims

You will see tables for tokens under your umbrella with:

- **Claimable** balances,
- **Claimed** history,
- actions such as **Claim**, **Sell**, **Transfer**, **Burn** when exposed.

Some flows are intentionally **two-step** (harvest then claim) to match on-chain accounting—read the button labels in the modal you are using.
