Every token has a treasury funded by a slice of trading fees (45% of swap fees, per the fee breakdown). Who controls the treasury depends on the trend's ownership type:

- **Creator trends** - the verified trend owner controls the treasury through the owner dashboard.
- **Community trends** - holders govern the treasury through Snapshot proposals.

## Where the Treasury tab lives

On token pages for verified trends, the **Treasury** tab shows the current balance, open proposals, vote counts, and execution status.

![Treasury tab figure](/images/token-treasury.svg)

## Proposal types

Three proposal types are supported:

- **Transfer** - send treasury assets to a recipient address for an approved purpose.
- **Burn** - destroy treasury-held tokens or route them to the burn mechanic configured for the trend.
- **Swap** - exchange treasury assets (for example, swap IP to USDC to de-risk).

## Snapshot and execution

Voting runs on **Snapshot**. The ip.world Treasury tab embeds the Snapshot proposal and lets you vote inline; the Snapshot link is available for the full proposal text.

Once a proposal passes, execution runs through the **Gnosis Safe multisig** linked from the proposal. The Safe signers finalize the on-chain action. Always confirm the Safe address shown in the proposal matches the trend's official treasury before signing or voting.

## Pass rule

A proposal passes when:

- **YES > NO**, and
- **YES ≥ 10% + NO** (baseline 10% plus one vote of YES per vote of NO).
