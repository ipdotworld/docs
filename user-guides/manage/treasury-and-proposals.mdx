## Where this lives

On supported **token** pages, open the **Treasury** tab to browse proposals, vote, or create proposals when your wallet qualifies.

Every token has a **treasury** funded by a slice of trading fees—**creator**-style launches route treasury control to owners; **community**-style launches route governance to holders (see [Community governance](/user-guides/manage/community-governance)).

![Treasury tab figure](/images/token-treasury.svg)

## Proposal types (Gitbook / product)

Common proposal families called out in product docs:

- **Transfer** — move treasury assets per approved text.
- **Burn** — destroy or route to burn mechanics as configured.
- **Swap** — exchange treasury assets (for example into stables or LP components).

Exact availability follows the live proposal composer.

## Snapshot and execution

Voting is powered by **Snapshot**; the UI may link out for full proposal text and results. **Execution** of passed actions is described in product copy as flowing through **Gnosis Safe** (or equivalent multisig) where configured—confirm the linked Safe from the proposal you are voting on.

## Proposal outcomes

Community math uses:

- **YES > NO**
- **YES ≥ REQUIRED_YES** where **REQUIRED_YES = 0.10 + 1 × NO** (baseline **10%** plus opposition scaling).

Same rules are documented in [Community governance](/user-guides/manage/community-governance).

## Shielded voting

If a space uses shielded voting, you might not see your plaintext vote echoed in-app—follow Snapshot’s UI.
