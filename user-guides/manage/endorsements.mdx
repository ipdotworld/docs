Endorsements let a verified trend owner signal which tokens under their trend are canonical, which they like, and which they disown. Endorsement chips show in the token header and in Explore lists.

## Who can endorse

Only **verified creator trend owners** can apply endorsements, and only on tokens under the trend they own. Community trends do not have endorsements - governance decisions on community trends run through Snapshot proposals instead.

## The three signals

- **Official token** - the canonical launch for the trend. Strongest positive endorsement.
- **Liked by owner** - a softer positive signal.
- **Flagged by owner** - the owner disputes the token; a warning for traders.

![Token header endorsements figure](/images/token-tabs-desktop.svg)

## For traders

Endorsements are owner signals, not investment advice. A flagged token can still have volume; an official endorsement does not guarantee price. Use endorsements alongside the chart, holders, and your own research.
