## Who can endorse

Only **verified creator trend owners** (post-claim) can apply endorsement chips to tokens under their umbrella.

## Signals

- **Official token** — strongest “this is the canonical launch” signal when used.
- **Liked by owner** — softer positive signal.
- **Flagged by owner** — warning that the owner disputes alignment.

![Token header endorsements figure](/images/token-tabs-desktop.svg)

## Trader interpretation

Endorsements are **signals**, not investment advice. Markets can still be volatile and rugs remain possible—pair social proof with your own research.
