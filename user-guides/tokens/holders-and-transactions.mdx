## Holders tab

The Holders tab shows every wallet that holds the token, ordered by balance. Each row shows the address (or username if resolved), balance, percent of supply, and PnL where available.

For tokens past graduation, the tab also shows:

- The current **holder reward pool** for the next distribution.
- The **window timer** until the next airdrop.
- Your **eligible balance** and **projected share**.

See [Holder rewards](/user-guides/rewards/holder-rewards).

![Holders tab figure](/images/token-holders.svg)

Empty state: **There aren't any holders right now** displays when the token has no holders yet.

## Transactions tab

The Transactions tab lists every swap against the token market - buy and sell, with wallet, size, price, and timestamp. The same transaction set powers the per-token PnL calculation on your profile.
