The token page is organized around a chart, a Trade panel, and a tab strip below them.

## Tabs

- **Feed** - indexed X and TikTok posts for this token.
- **Holders** - the top holders list and your holder-reward eligibility.
- **Transactions** - every swap against the token market.
- **Comments** - threaded discussion.
- **Treasury** - proposals and treasury balance for verified trends. See [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals).

![Token tab strip figure](/images/token-tabs-desktop.svg)

## How it works

The **How it works** button on the token header opens an explainer modal that walks through the fee split and reward flows for that specific token. Open it whenever you are unsure where a fee goes.

## Mobile

On small screens, **Trade** and **Comments** open as bottom sheets so the chart stays visible. Swipe down or tap outside the sheet to dismiss.
