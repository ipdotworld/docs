## Chart modes

The token chart supports viewing price history; some builds also expose a **market cap** view toggle—use the control in the chart header.

## Bonding curve and graduation

Before graduation, supply and price discovery are driven by the bonding curve UI (`bonding-curve` component). **Graduation %** shows progress toward migrating liquidity—exact threshold is **chain/config specific**; follow the number shown in UI rather than third-party writeups.

## Holder count

The chart panel surfaces **holders** alongside **graduation** for quick context.
