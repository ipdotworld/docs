## Chart modes

The token chart supports two views, toggled from the chart header:

- **Price** - historical price of the token.
- **Market cap** - price multiplied by circulating supply.

Timeframe controls on the chart let you switch between 5m, 1h, 1d, 1w, and all-time.

## Bonding curve and graduation

Before a token graduates, price discovery runs on a **bonding curve**. The curve determines price as a deterministic function of supply bought - early buyers pay less than later buyers.

The **Graduation %** indicator in the chart panel tracks progress toward graduation. When graduation hits 100%, the token migrates from the bonding curve into a deeper Uniswap V3 liquidity pool, and price is set by AMM trading from that point forward.

Graduation is also when UGC payouts and holder rewards turn on for the token.

## Holder count

The chart panel shows the current number of holders alongside graduation progress for quick context.
