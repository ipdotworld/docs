## Who can post

You must be **signed in** and have an active **wallet address** for the app to accept a comment. If you are not connected, the UI shows:

> **Please connect your wallet before submitting a comment**

## Replies

Threading is supported—open a thread to reply. Optimistic UI may show your message immediately, then reconcile with server state.

![Comments tab figure](/images/token-comments.svg)

## Empty thread

When no messages exist yet, the list shows **No comments yet**.
