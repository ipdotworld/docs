## What chains does ip.world support?

Two: **Story** and **Solana**. Every token market and every verification lives on Story. Solana is used for payments - you can pay or withdraw in SOL or SPL-USDC, and those funds bridge through Relay. See [Payment methods](/user-guides/trading/payment-methods).

## How much does it cost to launch a token?

**30 IP**, charged as an anti-spam fee on top of your initial buy. The fee is protocol-set and can change - the exact charge shows in the wallet prompt before you sign.

## How do I earn from UGC?

Link **X** and verify **TikTok**, then post about a token with the right signals - `$TICKER` on X, the token hashtag on TikTok. Posts that pass moderation and earn engagement split that token's UGC pool on Fridays. See [UGC rewards](/user-guides/rewards/ugc-program).

## How often are UGC payouts?

**Every Friday at 12:00 PM PDT.**

## How does verification work?

Creator trends: submit the five-step verification form from the trend page. Moderators review within 7 business days. Once approved, you claim the trend and activate your 3% allocation and fee share.

Community trends: no form - the trend verifies automatically when its top token hits $1M market cap, 1,000 holders, and 5–7 days of age.

## How do community treasuries work?

45% of every trade's swap fee flows into the trend's treasury. For community trends, holders govern the treasury through Snapshot proposals, with execution through a Gnosis Safe multisig. See [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals).

## What is anti-snipe?

An optional launch toggle that caps each external wallet at **2% of supply** for the **first 5 minutes** after launch. It blunts bots and reduces early concentration so the token distributes more evenly.

## How do holder rewards work?

Holders automatically share a cut of every trade's fees - **30% of the token-side fee** and **50% of the IP-side fee**. No staking, no locking. Larger holdings earn a larger share. See [Holder rewards](/user-guides/rewards/holder-rewards).

## Should I create a trend or a token?

If a trend already exists for the idea, launch a **token** under the existing trend. Create a new **trend** only when the idea has no home yet.

## Why does verification matter?

It proves ownership (for creator trends) or that the community hit the required milestones (for community trends), so fees route honestly. Verified creator trends unlock the owner dashboard and endorsements. Verified community trends unlock governance.

See [Verification overview](/user-guides/verification/verification-overview).

## What does READY FOR CLAIM mean?

The moderator-approved creator trend is waiting for the real owner to finish the claim. The owner needs a connected X or TikTok that matches the trend's owner link.

## Which wallet signs Story transactions?

Your **embedded EVM wallet on Story**, provisioned by Dynamic. The Solana embedded wallet handles SOL and SPL-USDC routing through Relay.

## What are the leaderboard tiers?

**Normie (≤500) → Casual (≤2,000) → Degen (≤6,000) → Trencher (≤15,000) → Legend (≤30,000) → GOAT (30,001+)**.

## How are points and fee-share different?

Points are an off-chain season score that rank you on the leaderboard. Fee-share is real on-chain earnings from trades. The same action (like a successful referral) can earn both.

See [Rewards overview](/user-guides/rewards/overview).
