## Should I create a trend or a token?

If the umbrella already exists and you are launching another market, create a **token** under it.

Read [Trends and tokens](/user-guides/trends-and-tokens) and [Launch a token](/user-guides/launch/create-a-token).

## Why does verification matter?

Verification reduces impersonation, unlocks **owner dashboards** and **endorsements** for creator umbrellas, and unlocks **governance** for verified community umbrellas.

[Verification overview](/user-guides/verification/verification-overview)

## What does **READY FOR CLAIM** mean?

The moderator-approved creator umbrella is waiting for the real owner to finish **Claim ownership** with a matching social login.

[Claiming a creator trend](/user-guides/verification/claiming-a-creator-trend)

## What is anti-snipe?

A launch option that caps per-wallet ownership for a short window after launch to reduce sniper concentration.

[Metadata, socials, and anti-snipe](/user-guides/launch/metadata-socials-and-antisnipe)

## What wallet signs Story transactions?

Your **embedded EVM wallet** on Story. Solana embedded is mainly for **Relay** funding routes.

[Embedded wallets](/user-guides/account/embedded-wallets)
