## What chains does ip.world support?

Markets and verification center on **Story** (EVM). The app also provisions a **Solana** embedded wallet and uses **Relay**-style routes so you can pay or receive **Solana-native assets** (for example **SOL**) where the trade panel exposes them—see [Payment methods](/user-guides/trading/payment-methods) and [Embedded wallets](/user-guides/account/embedded-wallets).

## How much does it cost to create a token?

Launches typically charge **`IP`** as shown in the wallet prompt—internal Gitbook copy cites **10 `IP`** as the anti-spam launch cost. Always **confirm the exact amount in-app** before you sign.

[Create a trend](/user-guides/launch/create-a-trend) · [Launch a token](/user-guides/launch/create-a-token)

## How do I earn from UGC?

Link **X** and/or **TikTok**, post content that follows indexing rules (**`$TICKER`** on X; token hashtag pattern on TikTok), stay above engagement floors, pass moderation, and meet pool eligibility (often post-bonding). Payouts are automatic when you rank in a funded weekly window—see [UGC rewards](/user-guides/rewards/ugc-program).

## How often are UGC payouts?

Product messaging targets **weekly** runs (example: **Friday, 12:00 PM PDT**). If any banner or modal in the app disagrees with docs, **trust the UI**.

## What is the verification process?

Submit evidence → **ip.world reviews** → on approval, **badges and economics** turn on. Creator umbrellas add a **claim** step for the real owner; community umbrellas lean on **milestones**. [Getting verified](/user-guides/verification/getting-verified)

## How do community treasuries work?

A slice of trading fees fills each token’s **treasury**. Holders propose and vote (**Snapshot**), then execution flows through a **Safe** multisig when configured. [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals)

## What is anti-snipe?

Optional launch setting that caps how much supply a **single wallet** can buy in the **first minutes** after launch (Gitbook: about **2% max per wallet for ~5 minutes**) to blunt snipers.

[Metadata, socials, and anti-snipe](/user-guides/launch/metadata-socials-and-antisnipe#anti-snipe)

## How do holder rewards work?

Holders earn **passive** fee share tied to **positions and volume**—product copy emphasizes **no staking / lockups** in the marketing story; engineering still uses windows and snapshots under the hood. [Holder rewards](/user-guides/rewards/holder-rewards)

## Should I create a trend or a token?

If the umbrella already exists and you are launching another market, create a **token** under it.

Read [Trends and tokens](/user-guides/trends-and-tokens) and [Launch a token](/user-guides/launch/create-a-token).

## Why does verification matter?

Verification reduces impersonation, unlocks **owner dashboards** and **endorsements** for creator umbrellas, and unlocks **governance** for verified community umbrellas.

[Verification overview](/user-guides/verification/verification-overview)

## What does **READY FOR CLAIM** mean?

The moderator-approved creator umbrella is waiting for the real owner to finish **Claim ownership** with a matching social login.

[Claiming a creator trend](/user-guides/verification/claiming-a-creator-trend)

## What wallet signs Story transactions?

Your **embedded EVM wallet** on Story. Solana embedded is mainly for **Relay** funding routes.

[Embedded wallets](/user-guides/account/embedded-wallets)
