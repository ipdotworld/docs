## Trend

The canonical page for an idea - a meme, a creator, a brand, a movement. A trend groups every token launched around the same idea. Older UI and engineering labels sometimes call this **IP**.

## Token

A single tradable market under a trend. Sometimes labeled **Remix** in older parts of the UI - same object.

## Remix

Legacy label for **token**.

## Creator trend

A trend with a clear real-world owner. Launching a token at creation is optional. Verified through the ownership-proof form.

## Community trend

A bottom-up trend owned by its holders. Launching a token at creation is required. Verified automatically when the top token hits the milestone thresholds (1M market cap, 1,000 holders, 5–7 days of age).

## Verification

The process that upgrades a trend from unverified to verified so ownership, economics, and governance are anchored to the right recipient.

## ITL (IP Token License)

The licensing framework that binds a verified creator trend to the owner. Read the PDF linked from IP Token License (ITL).

## IPA (IP Account)

A Story IP Account - an on-chain representation of an IP asset. Used in the advanced claim path when an owner already has an IPA registered for the IP.

## Endorsement

A signal a verified creator trend owner applies to a token under their trend: **Official**, **Liked**, or **Flagged**.

## Anti-snipe

Optional launch toggle. Caps each external wallet at **2% of supply** for the **first 5 minutes** after launch.

## Bonding curve

The pricing function used by a token before graduation. Price moves deterministically with supply bought.

## Graduation

The moment a token finishes its bonding curve and migrates into a Uniswap V3 liquidity pool. UGC payouts and holder rewards turn on at graduation.

## Canonical voting token

The first token created under a community trend. Holding this token gives you voting rights in the trend's governance.

## Governance pass rule

A proposal passes when **YES > NO** and **YES ≥ 10% + NO**.

## Points

The off-chain season score on ip.world. Drives leaderboard rank.

## Tier

Your leaderboard rank, based on total points: Normie, Casual, Degen, Trencher, Legend, or GOAT.

## Referral link

`https://ip.world?ref=YOURUSERNAME`.

## UGC (user-generated content)

Social posting program that pays creators from a cut of trading fees for posts that drive attention to a token.

## Holder rewards

Per-token fee share distributed to holders as airdrops. 30% of the token-side fee and 50% of the IP-side fee flow to holders.

## Relay

The cross-chain routing infrastructure ip.world uses to bridge SOL and SPL-USDC from Solana to Story before executing a trade.

## Embedded wallet

MPC wallet provisioned by Dynamic. Every ip.world account has two: one on Story (EVM), one on Solana.

## Virality

ip.world's 0–100 attention score for a trend or token. Powers the **Featured** sort in social feeds and the leaderboard's virality tier rewards.
