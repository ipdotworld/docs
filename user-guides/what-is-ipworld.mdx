## The ten-second version

**ip.world** turns internet culture into **trends** (canonical homes for an idea) and **tokens** (markets under that idea).

- If you are a **degen**: structured meme markets with charts, graduation, and fee rails that can pay holders and creators—not a naked DEX tile.
- If you are a **normie**: a place to follow a thing, see social context, and trade a specific launch when you are ready.

![Explore overview figure](/images/explore-default.svg)

## Two objects

- **Trend** — umbrella identity: name, art, socials, verification, and the list of tokens underneath.
- **Token** — one market: price, liquidity, trade panel, holders, and governance hooks when they apply.
