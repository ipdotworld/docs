## The ten-second version

**ip.world** is a launchpad for turning internet culture into markets. Anyone can create a token around a meme, trend, character, or idea. Trades generate fees that flow to creators, holders, communities, and the protocol—built for traders, creators, and anyone who participates in internet culture.

ip.world frames this category as **culture markets**: where internet culture and financial markets meet.

### What makes ip.world different

- **UGC rewards** — Content creators earn from trading fees when they post about tokens (for example on TikTok or X). Create content; get paid from the same fee rails that reward the market.
- **Community treasuries** — Each token has a treasury funded by trading fees. Holders govern how those funds are used (see [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals)).
- **Verification** — Real owners can verify their umbrella and tokens, unlocking fee routing, vested supply, and a trust signal in the app.

## Two objects (how the app is organized)

- **Trend** — the canonical home for an **idea** (meme, storyline, movement). One trend can carry **many tokens** underneath.
- **Token** — one **tradable market** under that idea: price, bonding curve, trade panel, holders, and governance when it applies.
