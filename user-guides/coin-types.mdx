## Creator-type tokens

Markets tied to a **single creator or brand** identity. Ownership, verification, and fee routing follow the **creator** path in the app when that flow is active.

## Community-type tokens

Markets where **holders** share direction-treasury and governance follow the **community** model in the app.

## Choosing in the app

When you launch, pick the type that matches how the market should be run. Labels and options in the **create** flow are the source of truth if this page and the UI ever differ.
