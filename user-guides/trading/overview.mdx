## Buy and sell

Open the **Trade** panel on a token page. Pick direction (**buy** or **sell**), pick the pay asset, enter the amount, and confirm in the wallet prompt.

Pay assets are **IP**, **USDC**, and **SOL**. ip.world trades settle on Story; when you pay with SOL or with USDC on Solana, the trade panel routes your funds through Relay before it executes the swap on Story.

![Trade panel figure](/images/trade-panel.svg)

## What the token page shows

Above the Trade panel, every token page shows:

- Market cap and price chart.
- Bonding curve progress toward graduation.
- Holder list and transaction history.
- Social feed and comments.

Check these surfaces before sizing a position.

## Where swap fees go

Swap fees are split between the trend owner, UGC creators, holders, the community treasury, and the protocol.

## Slippage and failures

When a trade fails, the revert or toast message tells you why. The common causes are:

- Not enough IP for gas.
- Not enough pay-token balance.
- You rejected the wallet prompt.
- The trade would exceed an active anti-snipe cap.
