## Direction

The Trade panel has a **Buy / Sell** toggle at the top. Switching direction flips the input and output fields and updates the slippage and fee estimates.

## Pay asset

Below the direction toggle, pick the asset you want to pay with: **IP**, **USDC** (Story or Solana), or **SOL**. When you pick SOL or Solana USDC, the panel shows a Relay quote - the quote includes the bridge hop to Story before the swap executes.
