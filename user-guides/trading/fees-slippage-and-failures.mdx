## Fees

Trading involves DEX and protocol fees; the UI surfaces totals before you confirm. For the **fee stack narrative** (treasury vs UGC vs holders), read [Tokenomics and fees](/user-guides/rewards/tokenomics-and-fees).

## Slippage

Set slippage according to your risk tolerance. Tight slippage on volatile tokens may cause reverts during large moves.

## Common failures

- **User rejected** — you cancelled in the wallet popup.
- **Insufficient funds** — add **IP** for gas or increase pay token balance.
- **Anti-snipe** — see [Anti-snipe](/user-guides/launch/metadata-socials-and-antisnipe).
- **Relay incomplete** — bridge flows can fail with “did not complete successfully” messages from relay helpers; funds may remain on source chain—read the error text carefully.
