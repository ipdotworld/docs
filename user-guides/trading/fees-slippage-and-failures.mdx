## Fees

Every trade pays a swap fee. The Trade panel shows the fee and your net amount before you confirm.

## Fee breakdown

Swap fees split across five destinations:

| Destination | Share |
| --- | ---: |
| Community treasury | 45% |
| Protocol | 25% |
| UGC creators | 15% |
| Liquidity | 12.5% |
| Referrals | 2.5% |

Community treasury, UGC, and referral shares are what reward the people behind the token. The protocol share funds ip.world itself, and the liquidity share is re-added to the pool.

## Slippage

Set slippage tolerance in the Trade panel before you confirm. A tight slippage setting on a volatile token can cause the transaction to revert when the price moves during the block - loosen slippage for large or late-mover trades.

## Common failures

- **User rejected** - you cancelled the wallet prompt.
- **Insufficient funds** - you need more **IP** for gas, or more of the pay asset you selected.
- **Anti-snipe limit exceeded** - the trade would push a wallet over the 2% cap inside the 5-minute anti-snipe window.
- **Relay did not complete** - a SOL or Solana-USDC route failed mid-bridge. Your funds stay on the source chain; read the error text, wait for the bridge to settle or retry, and do not sign a replacement until the original has resolved.
