ip.world trades settle on Story. You can pay with three assets: **IP**, **USDC**, and **SOL**.

## Story (direct)

- **IP** - the native gas token on Story and the default pay asset for token trades.
- **USDC on Story** - settles directly on Story; no bridge needed.

## Solana (through Relay)

- **SOL** - paid from your Solana embedded wallet. ip.world bridges your SOL to Story through Relay and then executes the swap.
- **USDC on Solana** - same path as SOL; bridged to Story through Relay before the swap.

When you pay with SOL or Solana USDC, the trade panel shows a Relay estimate before you sign. The actual amount you receive on Story can shift slightly between quote and execution because of market movement during the bridge - size accordingly.

## Where bridged funds land

If you sell a Story-native position into SOL, the SOL lands in your **Solana embedded wallet**. Manage it from the wallet menu in the app.

## Networks

ip.world uses two networks: **Story** for token markets and verification, and **Solana** for SOL and SPL-USDC routing. No other chains are supported inside the app.
