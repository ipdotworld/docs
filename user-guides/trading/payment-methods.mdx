## Story-native

- **IP** — native Story gas and pay asset.
- **USDC (Story)** — ERC-20 on Story at `STORY_USDC_ADDRESS` in `config/relay-chains.ts`.

## Solana via Relay

Relay supports Solana funding routes using Relay’s Solana chain id **`792703809`** (`RELAY_SOLANA_CHAIN_ID`):

- **SOL** — `SOLANA_NATIVE_SOL_ADDRESS` (`11111111111111111111111111111111`).
- **USDC** — SPL mint `EPjFWdd5AufqSSqeM2qN1xzybapC8G4wEGGkZwyTDt1v`.

Your **Solana embedded wallet** receives bridged funds when withdrawing Story IP to SOL via `relayStoryIpToSolana`.

## Chains

- **Story mainnet chain id** — **`1514`** (`RELAY_STORY_CHAIN_ID`).

## What is not implied

Other EVM chains may appear in curated Relay chain lists for future or partial support—**only document pay assets that the trade UI actually exposes** in your build.
