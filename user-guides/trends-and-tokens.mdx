## Trends

A **trend** is the canonical page for an idea: a meme, a creator storyline, a brand, a movement. A trend can have many tokens attached - different launches around the same idea.

Trends exist to:

- Group markets that belong together.
- Route fees and rewards to the right owner.
- Prevent duplicate pages and keep Explore coherent.

![Diagram: trends group multiple tokens](/images/explore-default.svg)

## Tokens

A **token** is a single tradable market under a trend. The token page is where the price chart, bonding curve, trade panel, holder list, and transaction history live.

If the app ever labels a token as **Remix**, they mean the same thing - Remix is the older name for a token.

## Why both exist

- **Degen framing:** a trend is the parent folder; the tokens underneath are the individual tickers.
- **Normie framing:** a trend is the topic page; the tokens are separate listings for different launches around that topic.

<Tip>
  When you open **Create**, you either start a **new trend** for an idea that does not exist yet, or **launch a token** under a trend that already exists.
</Tip>
