## When to use this flow

Use **Launch token** when the **trend (umbrella idea) already exists** and you are adding another tradable market under it. If no umbrella exists yet, start with [Create a trend](/user-guides/launch/create-a-trend) instead.

See [Trends and tokens](/user-guides/trends-and-tokens).

![Create token figure](/images/create-token.svg)

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Create">
    Click **Create**, then choose the flow for a **new token** under an existing trend (**Create token**; UI may still say **remix** in places).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link the umbrella">
    Search and select the **existing trend** you are launching under (the shared “idea” page for that culture).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Token identity">
    Enter **name**, **ticker** (`$TICKER`), **description**, and **image**; add **token socials** (includes **Telegram** where exposed).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Category">
    Pick the category that best matches the culture type—[Metadata, socials, and anti-snipe](/user-guides/launch/metadata-socials-and-antisnipe#category).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch options">
    Confirm any **Creator** vs **Community** or other toggles the form shows for this deployment (some builds inherit the parent trend’s model—follow the live screen).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Anti-snipe (optional)">
    Toggle early wallet caps—[Anti-snipe](/user-guides/launch/metadata-socials-and-antisnipe#anti-snipe).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initial purchase">
    Select creator opening buy in **`IP`** (roughly **0.1%–3%** of supply where the UI allows—confirm the slider).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay launch fee and confirm">
    Launches commonly charge **10 `IP`** as an anti-spam cost—confirm the exact number and recipient in the wallet prompt. Submit; the token should be **live and tradeable** once the transaction lands.
  </Step>
</Steps>
