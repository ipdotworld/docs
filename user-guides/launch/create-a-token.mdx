## When to use this flow

Use **Launch token** when the trend already exists and you are adding another tradable market under it. If the trend does not exist yet, create the trend first in the app — you can launch the token in the same flow.

![Create token figure](/images/create-token.svg)

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Create">
    Click **Create** in the nav.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the trend">
    Type the trend name. ip.world suggests existing trends as you type - pick the right one. Launching a new token under an existing trend keeps fees and ownership routed to the real owner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Token identity">
    Enter the **name**, **ticker** (`$TICKER`), **description**, and **image**. Add the token socials: X, Instagram, TikTok, Telegram, and a website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a category">
    Pick the category that fits the trend - Pet, Person, Trend, Game, Technology, Meme, Movement, Brand, Character, Art, or Misc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Anti-snipe (optional)">
    Toggle anti-snipe to cap each external wallet at **2% of supply for the first 5 minutes** after launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your initial buy">
    Enter how much you want to buy of your own token at launch. You can enter any amount denominated in IP, SOL, or USDC - the preset buttons (0.25%, 0.5%, 1%, 2%, 3% of supply) are shortcuts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay the launch fee and confirm">
    The launch fee is **30 IP**, added on top of your initial buy. Submit the transaction; your token is live and tradable as soon as the transaction lands.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The launch fee is protocol-set and can change. The wallet prompt always shows the exact charge before you sign.
</Note>
