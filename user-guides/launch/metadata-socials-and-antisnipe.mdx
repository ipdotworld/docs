## Trend fields

### Image

The primary card art for the trend page.

### Canonical name and aliases

The name is the canonical title of the trend. Aliases catch alternate spellings and common search variants, so people land on your trend even when they type it wrong.

<Tip>
  Add aliases the way people actually type the meme - abbreviations, misspellings, and slang all help.
</Tip>

### Ownership type

- **Creator trend** - a real-world IP with a single owner. The owner links X or TikTok during verification.
- **Community trend** - a crypto-native or bottom-up movement owned by holders. A token launch is required at creation.

### Category

Pick the category that best describes the trend. ip.world uses these eleven:

Pet, Person, Trend, Game, Technology, Meme, Movement, Brand, Character, Art, Misc.

If several fit, pick the strongest match. The category also determines which verification tracks the trend is eligible for later.

### Trend socials

Website, X, TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

## Token fields

### Image, name, ticker

The ticker is shown with a `$` prefix in feeds and search (e.g. `$MYTOKEN`).

### Token socials

Same as trend socials, plus Telegram.

## Copy trend detail to token

When you launch the first token for a new trend, the form pre-fills the token image, description, and socials from the trend. Override any field the token needs to market differently.

## Anti-snipe

Anti-snipe is an optional launch toggle that caps how much of the supply any single external wallet can hold during the first few minutes after launch. The cap is **2% of supply per wallet** for **5 minutes** from launch. It exists to blunt bots and snipers so the token can distribute more evenly.

Trades that would push a wallet over the cap during the anti-snipe window fail with an **Anti-snipe limit exceeded** error.

## Creator opening buy

Your opening buy is denominated in IP, SOL, or USDC and is free-form - enter any amount. The form also exposes quick-pick buttons at **0.25%, 0.5%, 1%, 2%, and 3%** of supply as convenience shortcuts. The panel shows the expected tokens received and the IP cost before you confirm.

The **30 IP launch fee** is added on top of your opening buy.
