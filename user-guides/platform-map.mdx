## Desktop

- **Explore** — scroll the feed, filter, sort, and open any **trend** or **token**.
- **Trend page** — the umbrella for an idea; lists tokens and ownership / verification context.
- **Token page** — charts, **Trade**, tabs (**Feed**, **Holders**, **Transactions**, **Comments**, **Treasury**), and **How it works** for economics context.
- **Create** — launch flows for trends and tokens.
- **Profile** — your identity, stats, referrals, and linked socials.
- **Leaderboard** — points, quests, and competitive rankings.
- **Settings** — username, bio, avatar, and social connections.

## Mobile

On small screens, **Trade** and **Comments** often open as **bottom sheets** so you can keep context on the token while acting. If a control is missing from the header, open the sheet—it is usually there.

## Where “money stuff” shows up

- **Trade panel** — swaps and payment assets.
- **Treasury** tab — proposals and community spend flows (when enabled for that token / trend).
- **Rewards** — points and referrals on **Leaderboard** and **Profile**; UGC and holder programs surface in token- and app-specific UI (see [Rewards overview](/user-guides/rewards/overview)).
