<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy the token">
    Open any token page, hit the trade panel, and buy. See [Trade coins](/user-guides/how-to/trade-coins) if you need a walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay above the threshold">
    Each token has a minimum holding threshold for eligibility. Check the **Holders** tab on the token page to see the current requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the reward window">
    Rewards distribute at the end of each reward window. Balances are snapshot during the window, and eligible wallets receive an airdrop when it closes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

That's it. No staking, no locking, no wrapping.

## Track your rewards

On any token page, look for the **Holder Rewards** widget — it shows your holdings, the payout timer, and rewards earned so far. Tap it to open the full modal with your projected share and claim history.

## Tips

- **Bigger bag = bigger share.** The pool distributes proportionally by balance.
- **More volume = bigger pool.** Active tokens with lots of trading generate more holder fees.
- **Don't sell below the threshold.** If your balance drops under the minimum during a window, you're ineligible for that round.
