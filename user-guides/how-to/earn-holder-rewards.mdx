## Read this first

Understand the rails in [Holder rewards](/user-guides/rewards/holder-rewards) (concept) and [Tokenomics and fees](/user-guides/rewards/tokenomics-and-fees) (where fees come from).

## In the app

1. **Buy and hold** the token in the wallet you use with ip.world.
2. Open the **token page** and check any **Holders** / rewards panels, countdowns, or history the UI exposes—[Holders and transactions](/user-guides/tokens/holders-and-transactions).
3. Higher **volume** on the market generally feeds larger pools over time; parameters can change—follow on-chain and in-app copy over static docs.
