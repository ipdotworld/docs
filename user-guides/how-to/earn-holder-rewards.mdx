## How to qualify

1. Buy the token and keep it in the wallet you use with ip.world. No staking or locking - holding is enough.
2. Stay above the holder-eligibility threshold shown on the token's **Holders** tab.
3. Wait for the next reward window to close. Payouts distribute as airdrops to the wallets holding at snapshot.

Holders receive **30% of the token-side trading fee** and **50% of the IP-side trading fee** on every trade. Higher trading volume grows the pool; larger holdings earn a larger share of that pool.

## Where to see it

On each token page:

- **Holders** tab - eligible balance, your projected share, and the window timer.
- Claim history for airdrops already distributed.
