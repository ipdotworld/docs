## Checklist

1. **Connect socials** in Settings — [Connect X](/user-guides/account/connecting-x), [Connect TikTok](/user-guides/account/connecting-tiktok) (required for UGC).
2. Post with the right **signals** (for example **`$TICKER`** on X and the token hashtag pattern on TikTok)—see [UGC rewards](/user-guides/rewards/ugc-program).
3. Pass **moderation** and meet **engagement floors** shown in live rules.
4. Confirm the token meets **pool eligibility** (often post-bonding)—still no guarantee of payout.

## Where it shows up

Indexed posts appear on the token **Feed** / UGC surfaces — [Social feeds](/user-guides/explore/social-feeds).
