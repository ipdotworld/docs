## Checklist

1. Connect **X** and verify **TikTok** in Settings - both are required to earn UGC rewards.
2. Post content with the right signals:
   - On X, include `$TICKER` in the post.
   - On TikTok, include the token hashtag shown on the token page.
3. Post content that passes moderation - no spam, plagiarism, or abuse.
4. Post about a token whose UGC pool is active. Pools activate after the token graduates from the bonding curve.

## Payout cadence

UGC pools distribute **every Friday at 12:00 PM PDT**. Top creators for each token share that week's pool, weighted by impressions and referral-driven volume. Payouts are automatic - no claim button in the default flow.

## Where posts show up

Indexed posts appear on the token **Feed** tab.
