Verifying a coin on ip.world means verifying the **trend** the coin lives under. Once the trend is verified and claimed, every token under it is verified too - you do not verify tokens one at a time.

## Before you start

You need:

- An ip.world account with a connected wallet.
- A connected **X** or verified **TikTok** account that matches the owner link on the trend.
- Supporting documents for ownership: social profile evidence, trademark or copyright registrations, license agreements, or proof of creation.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the trend page">
    Navigate to the trend (not the individual token) you want to verify. If the trend does not exist yet, create it first in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Verify">
    The trend page shows a **Verify** button when you are eligible. Click it to open the five-step form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the form">
    Work through the five steps: **Verification Eligibility → Supporting Information → Verifier Details → IP Rights → Terms & Conditions**. The **Verifier Details** step asks for your relationship to the IP - Original Creator, Authorized Representative, Community Lead, or Community Member - plus your name, email, and Telegram.     The **IP Rights** step is where you upload your supporting documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and wait for review">
    Submit the form. You will see a **Verification Submitted** confirmation. Moderators review within **7 business days** and contact you if they need more information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim the trend">
    When your request is approved, the trend page flips to **READY FOR CLAIM**. Click **Claim Ownership** and complete the wallet prompts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Community trends

Community trends do not go through this form. They verify automatically once the trend's top token hits the market-cap, holder-count, and age milestones.

## After you are verified

You receive the verification badge, the 3% vested allocation on every token under the trend, and your share of trading fees.
