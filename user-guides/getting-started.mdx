## Prerequisites

- A modern browser.
- A way to sign in: social (X, Google), email, or a wallet.
- Optional: **X** and **TikTok** accounts if you plan to earn UGC rewards.

## Funding your account

ip.world operates on **Story** and **Solana**. Supported pay assets are **IP** (Story), **USDC** (Story and Solana), and **SOL** (Solana).

Fund your account in one of three ways:

- Deposit from an external wallet (Phantom, MetaMask, or any wallet you already use).
- Swap between supported assets inside the trade panel - SOL and USDC cross-chain routes go through Relay.
- Use the on-ramp if it is available in your region.

For the full breakdown, see [Payment methods](/user-guides/trading/payment-methods).

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open ip.world">
    Go to [https://ip.world](https://ip.world).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Complete Dynamic sign-in (social, email, or wallet). ip.world provisions two **embedded wallets** for you - one on Story, one on Solana.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a username">
    Pick a public handle so referrals and the leaderboard resolve cleanly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link X and TikTok">
    Required to earn UGC rewards and complete onboarding quests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a token">
    Use **Explore** in the app to pick a token, then open its page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make a trade">
    Open the **Trade** panel, pick your pay asset, set the amount, and confirm. See [Trading overview](/user-guides/trading/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand rewards">
    Points, referrals, UGC, and holder rewards are separate programs. Read [Rewards overview](/user-guides/rewards/overview) before you start posting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If something breaks

- Refresh the page, sign out, and sign in again. Embedded wallet provisioning retries automatically on re-login.
- Ask in [Discord](https://discord.gg/ipworld) with what you clicked and what you expected to happen.
