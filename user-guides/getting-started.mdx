## Sign in

Go to [ip.world](https://ip.world) and sign in with **X**, **Google**, **email**, or an **external wallet** (MetaMask, Phantom, Rabby, etc.). Two embedded wallets — one on **Story**, one on **Solana** — are created for you automatically in the background.

## Onboarding

After sign-in, the app walks you through setup. Every step is **skippable** — you can come back to any of them later.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Referral code">
    Enter a referral code or `ip.world/?ref=` link to earn extra rewards alongside whoever invited you. Already landed via a ref link? The code was saved automatically — you can skip this step.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/quickstart-referral.png" alt="Referral code entry screen" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your profile">
    Pick a username, add a bio, and upload an avatar. Username is optional but required for the leaderboard and referrals to work properly.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/quickstart-profile.png" alt="Profile creation — username, bio, avatar" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect socials">
    Link your **X** and/or **TikTok** accounts. This is what qualifies you for **UGC rewards** — if you plan to earn from posting, do this now.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/quickstart-socials.png" alt="Connect X and TikTok accounts" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your wallet">
    Send **\$IP** or **\$USDC** to your Story wallet, or **\$SOL** / **\$USDC** to your Solana wallet. Scan the QR code, copy the address, or tap **Purchase directly** to buy with a card.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/quickstart-fund.png" alt="Fund wallet — QR code, address, and card purchase option" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Make your first trade

Open **Explore**, pick a token, and hit **Buy**. That's it — you're in.

For details on pay assets, slippage, and cross-chain swaps, see [Trading overview](/user-guides/trading/overview).
