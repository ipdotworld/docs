## Prerequisites

- A modern browser.
- A willingness to connect **social**, **email**, or a **wallet** (see [Sign-in methods](/user-guides/account/sign-in-methods)).
- Optional: **X** and **TikTok** if you want UGC or richer profile features later.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open ip.world">
    Go to [https://ip.world](https://ip.world).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Complete Dynamic sign-in (social → email → wallets). You will get **embedded wallets** on Story and Solana—read [Embedded wallets](/user-guides/account/embedded-wallets).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a username">
    Pick a public handle so referrals and leaderboards resolve cleanly—[Settings](/user-guides/account/settings-and-profile).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link socials (optional but powerful)">
    Connect **X** and verify **TikTok** if you care about UGC or social proof—[Connect X](/user-guides/account/connecting-x), [Connect TikTok](/user-guides/account/connecting-tiktok).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore and pick a token">
    Use Explore and open a **token page**—[Navigating tokens](/user-guides/explore/navigating-tokens).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make a trade">
    Open **Trade**, pick pay asset, confirm—[Trading overview](/user-guides/trading/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn the money rails">
    Skim [Rewards overview](/user-guides/rewards/overview) so you know **points vs referrals vs UGC vs holders** before you tweet about it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If something breaks

- Refresh, sign out, sign in again—embedded wallet provisioning retries automatically; see [Embedded wallets](/user-guides/account/embedded-wallets) for the logout fallback path.
- Still stuck? Ask in [Discord](https://discord.gg/ipworld) with what you clicked and what you expected.
