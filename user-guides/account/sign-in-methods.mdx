Signing in to ip.world unlocks trading, commenting, governance voting, trend verification, and points tied to your profile. Authentication is handled by Dynamic.

## Supported sign-in methods

- **X (Twitter)** - OAuth
- **Google** - OAuth
- **Email** - magic link
- **Web3 wallet** - EVM or Solana wallet

## Embedded wallets on first login

On first successful login, ip.world provisions two embedded wallets - one on **Story** (EVM) and one on **Solana**. You do not need to have an external wallet to start trading.

![Sign-in order overview](/images/auth-sign-in-order.svg)

## After you connect

The nav shows you as logged in. Open **Profile** and **Settings** from the account menu to set your username, link socials, and view your stats.

![Connected session overview](/images/auth-connected.svg)

## Sign out and switch accounts

Use the account menu to disconnect. Always disconnect before connecting a different wallet - leaving a stale session attached causes "wrong wallet" errors on transactions.
