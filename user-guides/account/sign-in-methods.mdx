## What signing in does

Signing in lets you **trade from embedded wallets**, **comment**, **govern** (when you hold voting tokens), **claim** verified trend ownership, and **earn** points tied to your profile.

## Supported sign-in types

Product copy groups the main paths as:

- **X (Twitter)** — OAuth
- **Google** — OAuth
- **Email** — magic link or password (per Dynamic configuration)
- **MetaMask** — external EVM wallet
- **Phantom** — external Solana wallet

Other wallets may appear in the same wallet list depending on Dynamic settings (for example **Rabby**).

## Order of options in the modal

The Dynamic modal is typically ordered as:

1. **Social** providers (one row).
2. **Or** separator.
3. **Email** sign-in.
4. **Or** separator.
5. **Wallet** list (external wallets).

## Embedded wallets on first login

On first successful login, the app provisions **embedded wallets** with both **Solana** and **EVM (Story)** addresses. No separate wallet install is required to start—see [Embedded wallets](/user-guides/account/embedded-wallets).

![Sign-in order overview](/images/auth-sign-in-order.svg)

## After you connect

You should see yourself as **logged in** in the chrome of the app and be able to open **Profile** and **Settings**.

![Connected session overview](/images/auth-connected.svg)

## Sign out and switch accounts

Use the account menu in the app to **disconnect**. If you need a different wallet, disconnect first—avoid “wrong wallet” errors on transactions.
