## What signing in does

Signing in lets you **trade from embedded wallets**, **comment**, **govern** (when you hold voting tokens), **claim** verified trend ownership, and **earn** points tied to your profile.

## Order of options in the modal

The Dynamic modal is configured in this order:

1. **Social** providers (one row).
2. **Or** separator.
3. **Email** sign-in.
4. **Or** separator.
5. **Wallet** list (external wallets).

Recommended external wallets called out in-product include **MetaMask**, **Phantom**, and **Rabby**—use whichever you already trust.

![Sign-in order overview](/images/auth-sign-in-order.svg)

## After you connect

You should see yourself as **logged in** in the chrome of the app and be able to open **Profile** and **Settings**.

![Connected session overview](/images/auth-connected.svg)

## Sign out and switch accounts

Use the account menu in the app to **disconnect**. If you need a different wallet, disconnect first—avoid “wrong wallet” errors on transactions.
