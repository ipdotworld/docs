## What you get

After sign-in, ip.world provisions **Dynamic MPC embedded wallets**:

- **EVM embedded wallet** on **Story** — this is your **canonical identity** for the app and the wallet that signs **Story** transactions (swaps, claims, governance txs prepared in-app).
- **Solana embedded wallet** — used as a **funding and exit route** for cross-chain **Relay** flows (for example bridging native **IP** on Story to **SOL** on Solana).

The client hook `useEmbeddedWallets` documents this split explicitly in code comments.

## Why Solana exists if Story is “home”

Some pay and withdraw paths use **Relay** with Solana-native assets (**SOL**, **USDC** on Solana). You still control keys via Dynamic; you are not asked to run a separate Solana browser wallet unless you choose a wallet-based sign-in path.

## Wagmi sync

Some flows require the active wagmi connector to match your embedded EVM address. If a button stays disabled after login, wait a beat, refresh, or toggle the active wallet from the Dynamic UI—then retry.

## Provisioning failures

If embedded wallets fail to appear after login, the app retries creation and may **log you out** with a toast so you can start clean. Signing in again usually resolves stuck provisioning.

## Export your embedded private key (Settings)

You can reveal the raw private key for each embedded wallet from **Settings → Security Settings** (tab label in the app: **Security Settings**).

There are two rows:

- **Story Wallet** — your embedded **EVM** address on Story.
- **Solana Wallet** — your embedded **Solana** address used for Relay funding paths.

Each row has **Show Recovery Key**. That opens a modal titled **IP Recovery Key** or **Solana Recovery Key** (matching the wallet you picked), powered by Dynamic’s `exportPrivateKey` flow. The key material is rendered into a container in the modal—often via a Dynamic **WaaS iframe** inside that surface.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Security Settings">
    Go to **Settings**, then choose **Security Settings** in the left nav (not Profile Settings).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the wallet">
    Under **Story Wallet** or **Solana Wallet**, click **Show Recovery Key**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm you are alone">
    Click **Reveal My Key**. A warning shelf appears: **Your key will be visible. Make sure no one is watching.** Use **Cancel** to back out, or continue only if you are in a private space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reveal">
    Click **I Understand, Reveal My Key**. While loading, the button shows **Revealing...** Dynamic then displays the export UI in the modal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy or hide">
    After the key appears, copy it only in a private environment—select the revealed text or use any copy control Dynamic surfaces inside the export UI. A **seconds countdown** appears in the key area (the view **auto-hides after 30 seconds**, with a thin progress bar along the bottom of the key box). Click **Hide Key** anytime to clear the surface early. Switching away from the tab (browser tab hidden) also clears the revealed key.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Anyone who sees your private key can drain that wallet. Never share it, never paste it into Discord or “support” forms, and never screenshot it where backups sync to the cloud. ip.world staff will never DM you for keys.
</Warning>
