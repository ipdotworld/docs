On first login, ip.world provisions two Dynamic MPC embedded wallets for your account:

- **Story wallet (EVM)** - your canonical identity on ip.world. Signs every trade, claim, and governance transaction on Story.
- **Solana wallet** - the funding and exit route for SOL and SPL-USDC. When you pay with SOL or withdraw to SOL, funds flow through this wallet via Relay.

Both wallets are controlled by Dynamic's MPC key management. You are not asked to install an external wallet to use ip.world.

## When Story actions stay disabled

Story transactions require Dynamic's **active wallet** to match your embedded Story address. If a trade or claim button stays disabled after you sign in, refresh the page or open the Dynamic wallet menu and reconnect, then retry.

## Provisioning failures

If the embedded wallets fail to appear after login, the app retries creation automatically. If it cannot recover, you are signed out with a toast so you can sign in again fresh.

## Export your private keys

You can export the raw private key for each embedded wallet from **Settings → Security Settings**. Two rows appear:

- **Story Wallet** - your embedded EVM address on Story.
- **Solana Wallet** - your embedded Solana address.

Each row has a **Show Recovery Key** button that opens a secure Dynamic export flow inside a hosted iframe.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Security Settings">
    Go to **Settings → Security Settings** in the left nav.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the wallet">
    Click **Show Recovery Key** on the row you want to export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the warning">
    Dynamic shows: **Your key will be visible. Make sure no one is watching.** Continue only if you are in a private space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reveal">
    Click **I Understand, Reveal My Key**. The button shows **Revealing...** while loading, then Dynamic renders the key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy or hide">
    Copy the key only in a private environment. The key **auto-hides after 30 seconds** (a progress bar below the key tracks the countdown). Click **Hide Key** to clear the screen early. Switching tabs also clears the key.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Anyone with your private key can drain that wallet. Never share it, never paste it into Discord or support forms, and never screenshot it. ip.world staff will never DM you for keys.
</Warning>
