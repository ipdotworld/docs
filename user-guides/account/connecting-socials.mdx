## Why connect socials?

For creator IPs, `ip.world` uses social identity as part of:

- figuring out who the likely owner is (owner suggestion / confirmation)
- letting the real owner claim the IP (when it’s ready)

## X (Twitter)

When connected, X is used to match you to a creator IP’s declared owner handle.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect X">
    Click the X connect button and complete the OAuth flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm connection">
    You should see your X handle reflected in Settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## TikTok verification

TikTok support is **verification-based**: you’ll see a status like pending / verified / rejected in Settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start verification">
    Click the TikTok verify button and follow the on-screen flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check status">
    Your status updates as pending / verified / rejected.
  </Step>
</Steps>
