Linking X lets ip.world:

- Attribute your X posts for UGC rewards eligibility.
- Display your X handle on your profile.
- Match your identity against owner links for trend verification.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings** in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start X linking">
    Click **Connect X** in the social connections section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve OAuth">
    Complete the X authorization in the popup window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Return to Settings. The **Connect X** button flips to a connected badge showing your handle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Disconnect

Click the disconnect control next to your X handle in Settings. You can reconnect a different X account after disconnecting.
