Settings has three sections: profile fields, security settings, and social connections.

## Profile fields

From **Settings**, you can edit:

- **Username** - your public handle. Also the `ref` code in your referral link.
- **Bio** - short free-text description on your profile.
- **Avatar** - image upload shown on your profile and in comments.

## Security Settings

**Settings → Security Settings** shows:

- Your **Story Wallet** (EVM) address.
- Your **Solana Wallet** address.
- A **Show Recovery Key** button on each row for exporting the private key.

## Social connections

Connect **X** and **TikTok** from Settings so they show on your profile:

- One account per platform at a time.
- You can disconnect and reconnect when you need to rotate accounts.
- Linking both is required to earn UGC rewards.
