## Where settings live

In the app, go to **Settings** (the page where you edit your profile).

<Note>
  **Screenshot**: Add a screenshot of the Settings page showing username/bio/avatar sections.
</Note>

## Edit your profile

In Settings you can update:

- **Username**
- **Bio**
- **Avatar**

<Tip>
  Keep your username consistent with your socials if you plan to claim or verify a creator IP later.
</Tip>

## Profile page

Your public profile page shows things like:

- your wallet address
- your username + bio
- your stats (including referrals)
