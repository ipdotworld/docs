Your profile is your public identity on ip.world. It surfaces your stats, holdings, activity, and referral link.

## Layout

The profile uses a two-panel layout:

**Summary (left)**

- Avatar, username, bio, and rank.
- Linked X and TikTok handles.
- Headline stats: points, tokens held, tokens launched, referrals, posts.
- Your referral link with one-click copy.
- Unrealized PnL across all positions.

**Main (right)**

- **Holdings** - tokens you created and tokens you hold, with market data and per-position PnL.
- **Activity** - your trades, launches, and comments.
- **Feed** - your indexed X and TikTok posts with engagement stats and UGC earnings.

## Tiers

Your rank on the leaderboard is one of six tiers: **Normie → Casual → Degen → Trencher → Legend → GOAT**.

## Tabs

Profile tabs: **Tokens**, **Trends**, **Takes**, **Referrals**, **Activity**.

## Referrals

Your referral link is `https://ip.world?ref=YOURUSERNAME`. The `ref` code is your username - keep your username stable so your attribution does not break.

## PnL

The profile shows per-token PnL, total unrealized PnL across your holdings, and shareable PnL cards you can post to X or TikTok.
