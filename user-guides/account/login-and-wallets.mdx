## What “login” means on ip.world

Logging in connects your wallet and creates a session so you can:

- create IPs/tokens
- vote / propose (community governance)
- claim ownership (creator IPs)
- edit your profile

## Connect your wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the connect flow">
    In the app, click the **Log in / Connect** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your wallet">
    Choose your wallet provider and approve the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm you're connected">
    You should see your connected status in the app UI and be able to access profile/settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common issues

- **The connect modal doesn’t open**: refresh and retry.
- **Wrong wallet**: disconnect and reconnect with the correct wallet (then re-check your profile page).
