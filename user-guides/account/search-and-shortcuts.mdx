## Open search

Click the magnifying glass in the nav, or press **⌘K** (macOS) / **Ctrl+K** (Windows/Linux) from anywhere in the app.

The search dialog covers three indexes at once:

- **People** - by username, display name, or wallet address.
- **Tokens** - by name, ticker, or alias.
- **Trends** - by name or alias.

## What to search for

- Type a ticker like `$TICKER` to jump to a token.
- Type a trend name to open the canonical trend page.
- Type a username or wallet to open a profile.
