## Creator trends

Once claimed, the verified owner has access to:

- **Owner dashboard** - harvest earnings, view vesting progress, manage endorsements, and take treasury actions.
- **Vested allocation** - 3% of every token's supply under the trend vests linearly over 3 months.
- **Fee share** - the owner's cut of trading fees on every token under the trend accrues continuously and is harvested from the dashboard.
- **Endorsements** - the owner can mark specific tokens as canonical.

## Community trends

Holders gain:

- **Governance** - submit proposals and vote through Snapshot.
- **Treasury** - proposals execute through a Gnosis Safe multisig funded by the community treasury share of trading fees. See [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals).

## Fees after verification

Verification changes who the trading fees route to, not how trades execute. Traders still trade the same markets.
