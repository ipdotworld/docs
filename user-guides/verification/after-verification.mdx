## Creator trends

Owners can access **dashboards** for holdings, claims, vesting, endorsements, and treasury-backed actions where enabled. See [Trend owner dashboard](/user-guides/manage/trend-owner-dashboard).

## Community trends

Token holders gain **governance** entrypoints: proposals, voting, treasury operations. See [Community governance](/user-guides/manage/community-governance) and [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals).

## Fees and rewards

Verification changes **who can sign** certain actions and **how fees route**, but trader-facing markets still behave like markets—read [Tokenomics and fees](/user-guides/rewards/tokenomics-and-fees) and [Rewards overview](/user-guides/rewards/overview).
