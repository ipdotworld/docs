## What is the ITL?

The **IP Token License (ITL)** is the licensing framework used by ip.world for **verified** umbrella identities (trends).

In-product copy references binding a verified umbrella to the ITL.

## Why it matters

ITL clarifies what verified **trend ownership** means for:

- markets launched under the umbrella,
- fee participation and attribution,
- how third parties should treat derivatives.
