## What the ITL is

The **IP Token License (ITL)** is the licensing framework ip.world uses for verified trends. When a creator trend is claimed, the owner and the trend are bound to the ITL so every token under the trend has a clear licensing basis.

## Why it matters

The ITL defines:

- Who owns the IP behind a verified trend.
- How trading fees are attributed back to the owner.
- How third parties should treat tokens and derivatives under the trend.

Without the ITL, there is no legal anchor tying the on-chain markets to the real-world IP.
