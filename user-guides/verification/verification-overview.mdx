Verification moves a trend from permissionless to trusted. It proves who owns the underlying idea (for creator trends) or that the community has hit the required milestones (for community trends), so the app can route fees to the right recipient and award a trust badge.

This page covers what verification is and what it unlocks.

## Two tracks

ip.world has two verification tracks, one for each ownership type:

- **Creator trend verification** - the real-world owner (or their authorized representative) proves ownership. The owner then claims the trend and unlocks owner economics.
- **Community trend verification** - the trend proves it hit the market-cap and activity milestones required for community status. Verified community trends unlock governance tooling for holders.

## What verification unlocks

For **both** tracks, verification unlocks:

- Fee routing to the owner or community treasury.
- The verification badge on the trend and all tokens under it.

For **creator trends**, verification additionally unlocks:

- **Vested allocation** - 3% of each token's supply, vested linearly over 3 months to the verified owner's wallet.
- **Owner fee share** - the owner's cut of trading fees on every token under the trend.
- **Endorsements** - the owner can endorse specific tokens under the trend as canonical.
- **Owner dashboard** - claim, harvest, and treasury controls.

For **community trends**, verification additionally unlocks:

- **Community treasury** - holders govern how the treasury is spent.
- **Governance** - proposals on Snapshot, execution through a Gnosis Safe multisig.
