## Why verification exists

Verification moves a market from **permissionless** to **trusted**: it confirms **ownership** (for creator-led culture) or **milestone truth** (for community-led culture) so the app can route fees honestly.

It keeps ip.world from turning into duplicate umbrella pages, fake owner claims, and mystery “official” signals.

## Two contexts

- **Creator trend verification** — proves the real-world or social owner so they can **claim** the umbrella and unlock owner economics.
- **Community trend verification** — proves the movement hit **market milestones** so token-holders can coordinate with governance tooling.

Read [Creator vs community trends](/user-guides/verification/creator-vs-community-trends).

## What unlocks after verification (Gitbook / product)

Across both models, verification is described as unlocking:

- **Fee routing** — owner or treasury paths activate per token economics.
- **Treasury control** — spending and proposals where applicable.
- **Vested supply** — owner allocations that unlock on schedule for verified creators.
- **Verification badge** — trust signal in UI.

**Creator tokens** — owner controls fees and treasury, receives vested allocation, earns from trading activity tied to owner rails.

**Community tokens** — treasury governed by holders; voting via **Snapshot**; execution via **Gnosis Safe** (see [Community governance](/user-guides/manage/community-governance)).

## Platform-specific unlocks

Depending on type, verification can also enable:

- **Owner claim flows** and earnings dashboards for creator trends,
- **Endorsements** on tokens under verified creator umbrellas,
- **Treasury + proposals** for community trends (token-holder gated).
