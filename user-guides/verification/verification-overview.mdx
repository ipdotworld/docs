## Why verification exists (the non-cringe reason)

Verification is how `ip.world` prevents the platform from becoming:

- 40 duplicate pages for the same meme
- fake owner claims
- unclear "official" signals that confuse users

It's basically **identity + accountability** layered onto a meme-native product.

## What "verified" can mean

There are two broad verification contexts:

- **Creator IP verification**: confirming the real-world/social owner of an IP and enabling them to claim it on-chain.
- **Community IP verification**: confirming that a community-run IP has met its verification criteria (based on on-chain / market metrics).

See: [Creator vs community IPs](/user-guides/verification/creator-vs-community-ips)

## What verification unlocks

Depending on the IP type, verification can unlock features like:

- creator ownership claiming (creator IPs)
- endorsements (creator-owned IPs can label tokens)
- community governance tools (community IPs, token-holder gated)
