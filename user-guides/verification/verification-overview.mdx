Verification proves who's behind a Trend. For creators, it's proof of ownership. For communities, it's proof of traction. Either way, it unlocks fee distribution, the verification badge, and governance tools.

Until a Trend is verified, **fees are locked up** — community treasury fees, owner fees, and referral fees don't distribute. Verification is what turns them on.

## Two tracks

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Creator verification" icon="crown">
    The real owner or authorized representative proves they control the IP. Can start **at any time** — no bonding requirement.

    Unlocks:
    - Owner fee share (10% of \$IP side trading fees)
    - 3% vested token allocation (linear over 3 months)
    - Ability to endorse tokens as **Official** or **Liked**
    - Owner dashboard
    - Community treasury for holders
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community verification" icon="users">
    A community lead or member proves the token has real traction. The token must be **bonded** (graduated) before verification can start.

    Unlocks:
    - Owner fees routed to the community treasury
    - The verifying token is automatically marked **Official**
    - Community treasury + governance (Snapshot + Safe)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What happens after approval

Once approved:

- A **Story Protocol IPA** and PIL are registered on-chain, with ownership assigned to the creator payout address (creator tokens) or the community treasury (community tokens).
- The Trend gets the **verification badge** — visible on the Trend and all tokens under it.
- **Locked fees are released** and start distributing immediately.
- **Creator tokens**: the owner can now endorse specific tokens as **Official** (full endorsement) or **Liked** (awareness without full endorsement) from the token management menu.
- **Community tokens**: the token that initiated verification is automatically marked **Official**.
- **Treasury tab unlocks** — holders can create and vote on proposals.

<Warning>
  Verification applies to the **Trend**, not individual tokens. Once a Trend is verified, all tokens under it are verified. You don't verify tokens one at a time.
</Warning>
