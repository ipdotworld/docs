## Creator IPs: how they get to “ready for claim”

Creator IPs typically move through these stages on the IP page:

1. **Identifying IP owner**
2. Owner socials may be suggested and confirmed/flagged by users
3. Moderator approval (the IP can become **READY FOR CLAIM**)
4. The real owner claims it (see below)

<Tip>
  If you know the owner of a creator IP, you can suggest their socials directly on the IP page.
</Tip>

## Creator IPs: how the owner becomes eligible to claim

To claim a creator IP, the app checks that:

- you are connected with a wallet session
- you have social identity connected/verified (X connected or TikTok verified)
- your connected identity matches the creator IP’s declared owner handle
- the IP has been approved and is ready for claim

See: [Claiming a creator IP](/user-guides/verification/claiming-a-creator-ip)

## Community IPs: how they become verified

Community IPs can show milestone-style verification progress on the IP page (e.g. market cap, holder count, age).

When a community IP is verified, governance tooling becomes relevant (token-holder gated).

See: [Community IP governance](/user-guides/manage/community-ip-governance)
