Verification is a five-step form you submit from the trend page. A moderator reviews the submission and responds within **7 business days**.

## The form

From a trend page, click **Verify**. The form walks you through five steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verification Eligibility">
    Confirm the trend is eligible for the track you are applying to - creator or community. The screen surfaces a summary of the trend and any prerequisites that are not met.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Supporting Information">
    Fill in the context behind the trend: category, owner link, and supporting evidence for why this trend represents the IP you are verifying.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verifier Details">
    Pick your relationship to the trend and fill in your contact info (name, email, Telegram). The four relationships are:

    - **Original Creator** - you are the person or entity behind the IP.
    - **Authorized Representative** - you represent a company or estate that owns the IP.
    - **Community Lead** - you lead the community around a community trend.
    - **Community Member** - you are a core participant in a community trend.

    If you pick Authorized Representative, you fill in the company name and your role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="IP Rights">
    Upload supporting documents: social profile screenshots, ownership proof (trademark, copyright, license agreement), or proof of creation. The form accepts multiple file types; provide whichever documents best prove your relationship to the IP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Terms & Conditions">
    Read and accept the verification terms, then submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After you submit

You see a **Verification Submitted** confirmation. The review team:

- Reviews your submission.
- Contacts you if more information is needed.
- Makes a decision within **7 business days**.
- Notifies you once the decision is made.

## Community trends

Community verification is triggered by on-chain milestones, not by manual upload. A community trend's top token needs to hit the required market-cap and activity thresholds before the trend is eligible; the trend page shows milestone progress as you move.
