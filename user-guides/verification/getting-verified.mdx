## At a glance (Gitbook / product)

1. **Submit** token / trend details plus **proof of ownership** (or milestone evidence for community paths).
2. **ip.world reviews** the submission (moderator / internal review).
3. On **approval**, verification state flips on and **badges + economics** activate per [Verification overview](/user-guides/verification/verification-overview).

Creator and community flows below add the UI-native detail those three steps summarize.

## Creator trends

Stages you will see on the trend page:

1. **Identifying owner**
2. Community may **suggest owner socials**
3. **Moderator approval** → **READY FOR CLAIM**
4. Owner completes **Claim ownership** — [Claiming a creator trend](/user-guides/verification/claiming-a-creator-trend)

<Tip>
  If you know the real owner, suggest their socials from the trend page.
</Tip>

### Eligibility checklist

- Wallet session connected
- **X** connected or **TikTok** verified
- Connected identity **matches** declared owner handle
- Trend marked **READY FOR CLAIM**

## Community trends

Milestone progress (market cap, holders, age) renders on the trend page; when satisfied, verification state updates and governance surfaces.

See [Community trend verification](/user-guides/verification/community-trends) and [Community governance](/user-guides/manage/community-governance).
