## When you can claim

On a creator IP page, you’ll see status text like **READY FOR CLAIM** when the IP is approved and claimable by the owner.

If you’re not the owner (or you haven’t connected/verified the matching social), you’ll be prompted to go to Settings first.

## Why claim?

The claim dialog explains the core reasons:

- **Earn rewards**: get **3% of all tokens vested over 3 months** and **50% of trading fees**
- **Get licensed**: the IP becomes bound to the IP Token License (ITL)
- **Grow your audience**: unlock creator-oriented growth mechanics

See: [IP Token License (ITL)](/user-guides/verification/ip-token-license-itl)

## Two claim paths

### Path A: Standard claim (most users)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your socials">
    In Settings, connect X or complete TikTok verification so your identity can be matched.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the IP page">
    Go to the creator IP page that shows **READY FOR CLAIM**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Claim Ownership">
    Click **Claim Ownership** and follow the on-screen steps + wallet prompts.
    This flow handles on-chain registration for you (creating/registering an IPA as part of the claim).
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Path B: Connect with an existing IPA (advanced)

If you already have a Story **IP Account (IPA)** registered, you can connect it during claim.

The UI will ask for your **IPA address** and verify that the connected wallet is the owner of the underlying NFT for that IP Account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open claim dialog">
    Click **Claim Ownership** on the IP page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose existing IPA">
    Click **Already have an IPA registered? Connect here**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste IPA address">
    Paste your IPA address. The UI verifies ownership automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim">
    If ownership is verified, click **Claim this IP**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
