Creator trends verify through a submission + review pipeline. The process has three stages:

## 1. Submit the verification request

The owner - or an authorized representative - opens the trend page and submits the five-step verification form.

## 2. Moderator review

ip.world moderators review the submission, verify documents and social ownership, and respond within **7 business days**. When the request is approved, the trend page flips to **READY FOR CLAIM**.

## 3. Owner claim

The verified owner claims the trend from their connected account. The claim flow compares your connected X or TikTok identity to the owner link on the trend - if they do not match, fix the connection in **Settings** before claiming.

## What the owner unlocks

Once claimed, the owner receives:

- **3% vested allocation** of every token under the trend, released linearly over 3 months.
- The **owner share** of trading fees on every token under the trend.
- **Endorsement** powers - the owner can mark specific tokens as canonical.
- The **owner dashboard** for harvesting, treasury management, and claim controls.
