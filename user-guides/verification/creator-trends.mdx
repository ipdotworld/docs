## Pipeline

1. **Owner signals** — suggested socials on the trend page.
2. **Moderator approval** — internal dashboard marks the trend **READY FOR CLAIM** when criteria are met.
3. **Owner claim** — the connected social identity must **match** the declared owner handle.

## Why OAuth must match

The claim flow compares your authenticated **X** or verified **TikTok** to the owner link stored at create time. If they do not match, fix connections in **Settings** first.
