Community trends verify when their top token hits a set of on-chain milestones. Unlike creator verification, there is no form to submit - the trend verifies automatically once the thresholds are met.

## Milestones

The trend's top token must reach:

- **$1,000,000** market cap
- **1,000** holders
- **5 to 7 days** of age on the platform

The trend page shows live progress toward each milestone. The exact thresholds are set by the protocol and can change.

## What happens after

When the milestones are satisfied, the trend flips to verified and unlocks:

- The community treasury address.
- Governance through Snapshot and a Gnosis Safe multisig.
- Fee routing to the community treasury.
- The verification badge.

Holders govern the treasury from this point forward — see [Treasury and proposals](/user-guides/manage/treasury-and-proposals).
