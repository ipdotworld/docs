## Milestones

Community umbrellas verify when their canonical token hits progress thresholds shown on the trend page. Product docs historically cite:

- **$1,000,000** market cap
- **1,000** holders
- **5–7 days** age

Those targets can change—**read the live milestone card**, not this page, before making financial decisions.

## What happens after

Verified community trends unlock **governance** flows: proposals, voting, treasury actions—see [Community governance](/user-guides/manage/community-governance).
