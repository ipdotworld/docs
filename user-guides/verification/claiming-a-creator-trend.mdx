Once moderators approve your verification request, the trend page shows **READY FOR CLAIM**. Claiming the trend registers ownership on-chain and activates your fee share, vested allocation, and owner dashboard.

## What claiming does

Claiming a trend:

- Issues the **IP Token License (ITL)** between you and the trend.
- Activates the **owner fee share** on every token under the trend.
- Starts the **3% vested allocation** on every token under the trend, released linearly over 3 months.
- Unlocks the **owner dashboard** for harvesting, endorsements, and treasury actions.

## Path A - Standard claim

Use this path if you do not already have a Story IP Account (IPA) for this IP.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect socials in Settings">
    In **Settings**, confirm your X or TikTok connection matches the owner link on the trend. The claim flow compares these before it lets you proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the trend page">
    Go to the creator trend that shows **READY FOR CLAIM**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Claim Ownership">
    Follow the in-app prompts. The flow registers the Story IPA for you and issues the ITL in the same transaction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm wallet prompts">
    Sign each wallet prompt. When the last transaction lands, the trend flips to verified and your dashboard activates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Path B - Connect an existing Story IPA

Use this path if you already own a Story IP Account (IPA) for this IP.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Claim Ownership">
    Click **Claim Ownership** from the trend page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Connect existing IPA">
    Pick the option to connect an existing IPA instead of registering a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the IPA address">
    The app verifies that your connected wallet owns the IP Account NFT for the address you paste.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the claim">
    Sign the wallet prompt to complete the claim.
  </Step>
</Steps>
