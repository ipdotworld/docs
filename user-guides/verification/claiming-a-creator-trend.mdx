## When you can claim

The trend page shows **READY FOR CLAIM** when moderators approve the umbrella and the owner may begin the claim dialog.

## Why claim?

The in-app claim dialog lists owner benefits such as **licensed** status under the **ITL**, fee participation, and growth tooling—read the live dialog for the exact strings shipped in your build.

See [IP Token License (ITL)](/user-guides/verification/ip-token-license-itl).

## Path A — Standard claim

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect socials">
    In **Settings**, connect **X** or complete **TikTok** verification so identity can match the owner link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the trend page">
    Visit the creator trend that shows **READY FOR CLAIM**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim ownership">
    Click **Claim Ownership** and follow prompts, including wallet transactions that register the Story-side IP account when applicable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Path B — Existing Story IPA (advanced)

If you already have a Story **IP Account (IPA)**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open claim dialog">
    Click **Claim Ownership**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose existing IPA">
    Use **Already have an IPA registered? Connect here** (wording per UI).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste IPA address">
    The app verifies that your connected wallet owns the underlying IP account NFT.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish claim">
    Confirm **Claim** when validation succeeds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
