---
title: "Creator vs community IPs"
description: "Two IP types, two different trust models"
---

## Creator IPs

Creator IPs are tied to a clear owner (an individual, brand, or project).

In the Create IP flow, creator IPs:

- can include an **optional owner link** (X or TikTok)
- have **optional** token launch at creation time

Creator verification focuses on identifying the real owner and enabling them to claim the IP.

## Community IPs

Community IPs are bottoms-up movements or trends.

In the Create IP flow, community IPs:

- **require** token launch (the UI forces "Launch Token" on)
- are treated as token-holder-owned
- can be verified via milestones like market cap, holder count, and age (the IP page shows progress)

Typical milestones are:

- **$1,000,000 market cap**
- **1,000 holders**
- **age threshold** (typically **5–7 days**)

## The key difference

<Tip>
  Creator IPs are about **who owns it**.  
  Community IPs are about **who coordinates it** (token holders).
</Tip>
