## Creator trends

**Creator trends** map to a clear owner (person, brand, or project).

- Optional **owner link** (X or TikTok) at create time.
- **Token launch is optional** at create time.
- Verification focuses on **who owns it** so the owner can claim and manage economics.

## Community trends

**Community trends** are bottoms-up movements.

- **Token launch is required** at create time (UI forces it on).
- Coordinated by **token holders**, not a single declared owner.
- Verification uses **milestones** such as market cap, holder count, and age (exact numbers on the trend page).

Typical milestone targets discussed in product docs include **$1,000,000 market cap**, **1,000 holders**, and **5–7 days** age—the **live progress card** always wins if numbers differ.

## The key difference

<Tip>
  **Creator** = who owns the umbrella. **Community** = who coordinates it once tokens exist.
</Tip>
